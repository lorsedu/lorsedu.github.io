10. Question for Day 1 - Dovie, Resthel and Cherie went to a mall. Dovie spent ₱178 more than Resthel but ₱52 less than Cherie. If Dovie spent ₱1284, what was the total amount of money the girls spent in the mall?


Answer: ₱3726.00

Explanation:

Dovie - 178= Resthel

(This means Dovie spent ₱178 more than Resthel.)

Dovie+52=Cherie

(This means Dovie spent ₱52 less than Cherie.)

Dovie=₱1284

(This means Dovie spent ₱1284.)

Solution:

Now lets get the amount of Resthel spent.

D(Dovie) ₱1284.00 -₱178.00=₱1106.00

So it means Resthel spent ₱1106.00

Now lets get the amount of Cherie spent.

D(Dovie) ₱1284.00+ ₱52.00=₱1336.00

So it means Cherie spent ₱1336.00

And lastly add the amount of the three spent.

D+R+C= ₱1284+₱1106+₱1336=₱3726.00

So, the answer is ₱3726.00

16. Assignment 1 What is I rony?​


Answer:

Irony is a literary device in which contradictory statements or situations reveal a reality that is different from what appears to be true. There are many forms of irony featured in literature. The effectiveness of irony as a literary device depends on the reader’s expectations and understanding of the disparity between what “should” happen and what “actually” happens in a literary work. This can be in the form of an unforeseen outcome of an event, a character’s unanticipated behavior, or something incongruous that is said.

One of the most famous examples of irony in literature comes from The Gift of the Magi by O. Henry. In this story, a newly married couple decides independently to sacrifice and sell what means most to themselves in order to purchase a Christmas gift for the other. Unfortunately, the gifts they receive from each other are intended for the very prized possessions they both sold. As a result, though their sacrifices symbolize the love they have for each other, the actual gifts they receive are all but useless.

26. Dovie,Resthel,and Cherie went to a mall. Dovie spent 178 pesos more than Resthel but 52 pesos less than Cherie. If Dovie spent 1284 pesos,what was the total amount of money the girl spent in the mall?


Answer:

The total amount of money the girls spent in the mall is 3,726 pesos.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us analyze the problem.

What is asked?

The total amount of money the girls spent in the mall.

What are the given?

amount of money Dovie spent - 178 pesos more than Resthel but 52 pesos less than Cherie or 1284 pesos

There are three steps to find the total amount of money the girls spent in the mall.

1. Find the amount of money spent by Resthel.

2. Find the amount of money spent by Cherie.

3. Add all the amount of money they spent.

Equations:

Step #1

"Dovie spent 178 pesos more than Resthel"

To find the amount of money Resthel spent, we need to take away 178 pesos from the amount of money Dovie spent because based on the problem Resthel spent less than Dovie by 178 pesos.

1,284 pesos - 178 pesos = N

Step #2

"Dovie spent 52 pesos less than Cherie"

To find the amount of money Cherie spent, we need to add 52 pesos to the amount of money Dovie spent because based on the problem Cherie spent more than Dovie by 52 pesos.

1,284 pesos + 52 pesos = N

Solution:

Step #1

Resthel's amount of money spent

= 1,284 pesos - 178 pesos

= 1106 pesos

Resthel spent 1,106 pesos.

Step #2

Cherie's amount of money spent

= 1,284 pesos + 52 pesos

= 1,336 pesos

Cherie spent 1,336 pesos.

Step #3

1,284 pesos + 1,106 pesos + 1,336 pesos = 3,726 pesos

Final Answer:

The total amount of money the girls spent in the mall is 3,726 pesos.

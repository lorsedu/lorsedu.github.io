11. X LUISA is thrice as old as BETH. If Beth is M years old, Len is 3 years older than BETH4.Expresss Len's age algebraically _________________5.Write the expression for the sum of LUISA and BETH age.________________​


Answer:

4. Len's age algebraically is M + 3.

5. The expression for the sum of Luisa and Beth's age is 3M + M = 4M.

Explanation:

According to the problem, Luisa is thrice as old as Beth. This means that Luisa's age is 3 times Beth's age or 3M (where M is Beth's age).The sum of Luisa and Beth's age is simply the sum of their respective ages, which is 3M (Luisa's age) + M (Beth's age) = 4M.The problem also states that Len is 3 years older than Beth. Therefore, Len's age can be expressed algebraically as M + 3 (where M is Beth's age).

Personal Pronouns

Personal pronouns are short words used as simple substitutes for the proper name of a person. Each personal pronouns shows the grammatical person, gender, number, and case of the noun it replaces. Examples are I, you, he, she, it, we they, me, him, her, us, and them. The stunt doubles of grammar; they stand in for the people (and perhaps animals) who star in our sentences.

27. why is san roque national high school named after san roque in antipolo city?​


Answer:

San Roque National High School is likely named after San Roque, who is a Catholic saint widely venerated in the Philippines. San Roque is the patron saint of the sick, the poor, and of those who suffer from infectious diseases. The name San Roque is commonly associated with healing and protection, which could be a reason why the school was named after the saint to symbolize the institution's commitment to providing education and support to its students. As for why it was named specifically after San Roque in Antipolo City, it's possible that the school was established in honor of a local religious tradition or a historical event that is associated with the saint in that area.

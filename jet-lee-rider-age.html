Answer:

for number 2.

Mr. Lee is 36 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Name: Representation: Second Rep.

Mr. Lee. x x/3

Mr. park x+12 (x+12)/4

(x+12)/4 = x/3

3(x+12) = 4(x)

3x+36 =4x

3x-4x= -36

-x = -36

-1(-x)= (-36)-1

x = 36

30. which has a greater momentum? a bicycle with a 30 kg rider or a tricycle with a 30 kg rider​


Answer:

A greater momentum is a tricycle with a 30 kg rider.

Explanation:

The more momentum a moving object has, the harder it is to stop. The mass of an object affects the amount of momentum the object has. For example, you can catch a baseball moving at 20 m/s, but you cannot stop a car moving at the same speed. The car has more momentum because it has a greater mass.

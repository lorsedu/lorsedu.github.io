Fossil Evidence

19. express0.3178bar in form aby b​


Answer:

338/909

Step-by-step explanation:

Bar indicates that the decimal is repeating, hence,

[tex]0.\overline{3718} = 0.371837183718...[/tex]

Repeating decimals can be expressed in fractions with the denominator having as many 9s as the repeated part.

[tex]0.\overline{3718} = 3718/9999 = 338/909[/tex]

Therefore, the decimal can be represented as 338/909 in lowest terms.

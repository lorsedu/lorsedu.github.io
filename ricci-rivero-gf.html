Answer:

Matteo Ricci (Italian pronunciation: [matˈtɛːo ˈrittʃi]; Latin: Mattheus Riccius Maceratensis; 6 October 1552 – 11 May 1610), was an Italian Jesuit priest and one of the founding figures of the Jesuit China missions. Answer: total = 761

average height = 76.1 inches

Step-by-step explanation:

total = 81 + 78 + 71 + 75 + 70 + 76 + 77 + 80 + 79 + 74

       = 761

average height = 761 ÷ 10

                          = 76.1 inches

In order to get the average height of the River Family, you will need to add all of their heights then divide it by the total members of the family.

Average = Total height ÷ Number of family members

Total height = 81+78+71+75+70+76+77+80+79+74

Total height = 761 inches

Number of Family members = 10

Solution:

Average = Total height ÷ Number of family members

Average = 761/10

Average = 76.1 inches

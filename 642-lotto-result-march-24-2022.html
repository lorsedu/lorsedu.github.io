In a 6/42 lotto game, how many different bets are possible​?​

5,245,786Solution:

Get the combination of 6 numbers from set of 42 numbers.

C(42,6)

using combination formula

[tex]\sf C(n,r) = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}\\\\\sf C(42,6) = \frac{42!}{6!(42-6)!}\\\\\sf C(42,6) = \frac{42!}{6!36!}\\\\\sf C(42,6) = \frac{42\times41\times40\times39\times38\times37\times36!}{6! 36!}\\\\ \text{\sf 36! cancels out}\\\\\sf C(42,6) = \frac{42\times41\times40\times39\times38\times37\times\cancel{36!}}{6! \times \cancel{36!}}\\\\\sf C(42,6) = \frac{42\times41\times40\times39\times38\times37}{6 \times 5\times4\times3\times2\times1}\\\\\sf C(42,6) = 5,245,786[/tex]

[tex]\therefore \boxed{\text{\sf There are 5,245,786 possible bet combinations.}}[/tex]

10. when we see a red shirt what color(or colors) of light is the shirt reflecting? A. Red. B.Red and green. C.All of the above. D.Blue and yellow​


RED SHIRT

When we see a red shirt what color(or colors) of light is the shirt reflecting?

A. Red.

B.Red and green.

C.All of the above.

D.Blue and yellow

ANSWER

Amongst the given choices above the correct answer is letter A. Red.

This is the best example of the concept of reflection.

Light makes objects become more vibrant in color, this is because light actually has a range of colors, when light reaches the red shirt, it will absorb all the colors in visible light spectrum (orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet) except color red, because the red shirt will bounce back or reflect the red color.

This is why the color of shirt becomes more red when shone by light.

14. 4. Which of the following would absorb the most sunlight? A. A white t-shirt B. An orange t-shirtC. A yellow t-shirt D. A black t-shirt ​


Answer:

A black t-shirt would absorb the most sunlight. Darker colors tend to absorb more light than lighter colors, and black is the darkest color. The absorbed light energy is converted into heat energy, which is why black clothing can become much hotter in the sun than lighter colored clothing.

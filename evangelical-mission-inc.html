5. how can the Lay apostolate fulfill it's mission as evangelizers of the church​


Answer:

The lay apostolate is made up of laypersons, who are neither consecrated religious nor in Holy Orders, who exercise a ministry within the Catholic Church. Lay apostolate organizations operate under the general oversight of pastors and bishops, but need not be dependent upon them for direction.

The laity can exercise a fruitful apostolate by their conduct in the areas of their labor, profession, studies, neighborhood, and social life. 27. The Church exists to _________. Choices: A. evangelize B. apostles C. Mission D. Jesus E. Kingly F. Ekklesia G. Priestly H. duty I. Prophetic J. Father


Answer:

The Church exists to evangelize.

Evangelize means to spread the gospel, or the message of Jesus Christ and his teachings, to others. The Church, which is the body of believers in Jesus Christ, exists for the purpose of spreading the gospel and sharing the message of salvation through Jesus with the world. This is often referred to as the "Great Commission," as Jesus himself instructed his followers to go out into the world and make disciples of all nations (Matthew 28:19-20).

Other possible answers might include "mission" (which could refer to the Church's mission to evangelize and spread the gospel), "Jesus" (as the Church exists to follow Jesus and carry out his teachings), and "priestly" (as the Church is made up of priests who serve and minister to the body of believers). However, "evangelize" is the best answer in terms of describing the overall purpose of the Church.

15. paul, ben, and emma had some stickers. ben had 200 more stickers than paul. the ratio of the number of stickers paul had to the number of stickers emma had was 4:3. after ben gave 1/8 of his stickers to paul, he had 60 fewer stickers than paul. find the number of stickers paul. had at first?​


Ratio and Proportion

The problem is an application of ratio and proportion. Let's define, ratio and proportion.

What is Ratio

A ratio is an ordered pair of numbers a and b, written as a / b or a : b, where a and b does not equal to zero.

Example

1/2 or 1:2, read as one is to two

3/4 or 3:4, read as three is to four

6/5 or 6:5, read as six is to five

In example number one, it states that in every 1 unit, it has 2 units or item. Like, in a class, in every 1 boy, it has 2 girls in a group presentation. The ratio of boy to girl is 1:2.

Remember that, ratio is always written in lowest term.

What is Proportion

A proportion is an equation in which two ratios are set equal to each other.

Example

2/3 = 4/6

1/3 = 3/9

4/7 = 8/14

You can identify if the set of ratio are proportion when the cross product is equal.

2/3 = 4/6

Cross Product → (2)(6) = (3)(4) → 12 = 12 ✓

In the given problem "Paul, ben, and emma had some stickers. ben had 200 more stickers than paul. the ratio of the number of stickers paul had to the number of stickers emma had was 4:3. after ben gave 1/8 of his stickers to paul, he had 60 fewer stickers than paul. find the number of stickers paul. had at first?"

Given

Ben had 200 more stickers than Paul

The ratio of the number of stickers paul had to the number of stickers emma had was 4:3

Ben gave 1/8 of his stickers to paul, he had 60 fewer stickers than Paul

Solution

Let x is the number of Paul's stickers

Ben = 200 + x

4:3 = x:y, where y is the number of Emma's stickers

y = 3x/4

1/8(200+x) + x = 7/8(200+x) + 60

Solve for x

1/8(200+x) + x = 7/8(200+x) + 60

25 + 1/8x + x = 175 + 7/8x + 60

1/8x + x - 7/8x = 175 + 60 - 25

1/4x = 210

x = 840

Check

Ben = 200 + x = 840 + 200 = 1040

Paul has 840 stickers and Ben has 1040 stickers.

Ben gave 1/8 of his stickers to paul, he had 60 fewer stickers than Paul.

1/8(1040) + 840 = 970→ new stickers of Paul

1040 - 1/8(1040) = 910 → present stickers of Ben

910 is 60 fewer than 970, (CORRECT)

Final answer

The number of stickers Paul is 840.

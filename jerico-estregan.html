13. Leo hiked 6/7 of a kilometer. Jerico hiked 2/3 kilometer. Who covered a longer distance? How much longer


Answer:

Leo covered 0.190 km more than Jericho

Step-by-step explanation:

Given;

Leo hiked 6/7 of a kilometer

Jericho hiked 2/3 of a kilometer

Find; Who covered a longer distance? How much longer?

Solution;

6/7 , 2/3

Lcm of (7 , 3) is 21

6/7 × 2/3 = 18/21

2/3 × 7/7 = 14/21

18/21 > 14/21

6/7 > 2/3

Leo covered longer distance than Jericho?

6/7 - 2/3 = 6×3-2×7/21 = 18-14/21 = 4/21

=0.190 km

hence; Leo covered 0.190 km more than Jericho

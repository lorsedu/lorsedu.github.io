3. sino si Maximo Hizon​


Answer:

Si Maximino H. Hizon ay ipinanganak noong ika-9 ng Mayo, 1870 sa bayan ng Mexico, Pampanga. Ang kanyang mga magulang ay sina Atanacio Hizon at Ancieta Hipolito.

Unang nag-aral si Maximino sa Pampanga, at ito ay kanyang ipinagpatuloy sa Maynila.

Nang nas hustong gulang na ay sumanib si Hizon sa lihim na samahang rebolusyonaryo. Siya rin ang nagtatag ng ganitong samahan sa kanyang bayan sa Mexico, Pampanga. Dahil dito, siya ay nakulong, ngunit siya ay pinalaya rin matapos ang kasunduan sa Biak-na-Bato. Muli siyang sumanib sa grupo ng mga rebelyonista noong taong 1898. Pinamunuan niya ang mga pag-atake sa garison ng mga Kastila sa bayan ng San Fernando, Pampanga.

Siya ay inatake sa puso at namatay noong Setyembre 1, 1901.

4. Mga katangian ni Lolita Hizon​


Answer:

Talambuhay ni lolita hizon

Explanation:

Ang talambuhay ni Lolita Hizon ay nakalahad sa pamamagitan ng simpleng pamamaraan sa ibaba:

Si Lolita Hizon ay kilala rin bilang "Apung Lolet" sa Kapampangan.

Siya ang babae sa likod ng sikat na Pampanga's Best na may produktong tocino, longganisa, bacon, hotdog, at marami pang iba.

Bago pa man sumikat ang Pampanga's Best, siya ay isang guro sa elementarya.

Inamin niyang wala siya sa pokus tuwing nasa klase dahil iniisip niya kung nasaan ang kanyang asawa na may negosyong trucking at gasolinahan. Naisip niyang hindi sulit ang binabayad sa kanya ng paaralan dahil wala siya sa pokus tuwing nasa klase.

Paano nagsimula ang Pampanga's Best? Pinagdasal niya na bigyan siya ng isang trabaho na sulit ang pasahod sa kanya. Narito ang sikat na "Apung Lolet story": Nagsimula ang tagumpay niya noong ang kanyang kapitbahay, na nagtitinda ng karne sa palengke, ay may tirang paninda. Nag-volunteer si Apung Lolet na gawan ng timpla ang karne upang mapakinabangan pa ito at tumagal ang buhay ng karneng baboy.

Naisip ni Apung Lolet na gamitin ang orihinal na sangkap upang kumita. Gamit ang P3,000 na kapital, napalago niya ang kanyang negosyo na nagsimula sa San Fernando Pampanga Public Market. Ang negosyong ito ay ang kilalang "Pampanga's Best" na ngayon.

7. Lolita Hizon'sfamily-education-training-


Answer:

The story goes that Mrs. Lolita O. Hizon neighbor, a meat vendor, had some unsold pork at the end of a market day and, not wanting to let these spoil, asked Mrs. Hizon’s help in cooking them. Mrs. Hizon came up with a formula to cure the meat; she revised the traditional Capampangan pindang (fermented pork), causing the pork to acquire the unique salty – sweet taste that we have all come to love. She called it “Tocino”, derived from a Spanish delicacy that is sweet. Eventually, she refined the formula and the processing procedures that caused the birth of Pampanga’s Best Tocino – The Original version of what has now become a national favorite. So, what started out as just a neighborly gesture to help a friend has turned out to be the cornerstone of a business and a staple item in the Filipino breakfast table.

When Mrs. Lolita O. Hizon, is asked what she thinks is the secret behind her success she says, It’s love and compassion; and I believe it is more of a divine plan. All these years, I see a visible hand guiding us always for a purpose. I would say it is Divine Providence – that is the secret.

Today, Pampanga’s Best, Inc. is a multi-million meat processing corporation, operated and owned by the couple, Mr. Angelo D. Hizon Jr. and Mrs. Lolita O. Hizon, and their twelve children. It manufactures eleven core products – Tocino, Hotdogs, Longaniza, Ham, Bacon, Tapa, Burger Patties, Corned Beef, Embotido, Barbecue, and Nuggets.

True to it’s motto – Always the best from Pampanga’s BEST – the company puts prominence on the importance of high quality throughout it’s more than 40 years in the business, creating customer value, satisfaction, and loyalty. Also, it acquired the AAA Category, the highest level for a manufacturing plant, from the National Meat Inspection Service.

Consumers’ taste for something new is endless, which leads to the development of new products based on the consumers’ preference and his environment. With the expertise and creativity of its Research and Development Team, the company is able to cater to the different needs and demand of the consumers.

Competition is growing so fast – entrants of new and small enterprises and some companies are imitating our products, but with the company’s mission to adopt new technologies for the innovation of its products and service and its creatively and uniquely designed and well – executed systems, Pampanga’s Best is surely a brand to match; because the value it gives to it’s customers is irreplaceable.

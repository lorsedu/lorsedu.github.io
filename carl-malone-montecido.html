15. Carl’s father is five times as old as Carl. In 2 years, Carl will be one-fourth as old as his father. How old is Carl’s father now?​


Answer:

Carl is 6 years old now but in 2 years he will be 8

Step-by-step explanation:

6×5=30

6+2=8

30+2=32

32÷8=4

5*(2÷1/4)
=5*(2*4)
=5*8
Carls father in two years=40

If Chiky is 39 now and Cardo is 4 years older, then Cardo should be 35 now. If Mocang is 16 years older than Cardo, then Mocang should be 51 now. And if Tanggol is 4 years older than Mocang, then Tanggol should be 55 now.

Let's check if these ages satisfy the original conditions:

Tanggol is 4 years older than Mocang: 55 - 4 = 51 (Mocang's age)

Mocang is 16 years older than Cardo: 51 - 16 = 35 (Cardo's age)

Cardo is 4 years older than Chiky: 35 - 4 = 31 (Chiky's age)

These ages do work! So the solutions for this problems are:

Tanggol is 55 years old

Mocang is 51 years old

Cardo is 35 years old

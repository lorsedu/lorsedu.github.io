15. How long will it take for Php 800 to earn Php 240, if it is invested at 6 ½ % simple interest?​.


This problem can be solved by using the formula I = PRT where, I is the interest, P is the principal amount, R is the rate, and T is the time.

The given in the problem are

P = 800

1 = 240

R = 6.5% or 0.065

What is asked in the problem is the time T We will now solve the problem by substituting the given to the formula

| = PRT

240 = 800(0.065)(T)

240 = 52T

240/5252T/52

4.62 = T

T = 4.62 years

Therefore, it will take 4.62 years for 800 pesos to earn 240 pesos at 6.5% interest rate

1. john borrowed 30,000 from paul who charged 18% interest rate. how much would she pay to paul after 6 months.​


Answer:

John will pay 32,700

Step-by-step explanation:

Identify: Total amount to pay

Let,

A = total amountI = interest earned

Given:

P = 30,000r = 18%t = 6 mos.

Formula:

I = PrtA = P + I

Solve:

I = Prt

= 30,000(0.18)(6/12)

= 30,000(0.09)

I = 2,700

A = P + I

= 30,000 + 2,700

A = 32,700

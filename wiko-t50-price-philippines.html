19. if Wiko has an unknown quantity of gas at a pressure of 3.25 atm, a volume of 29 liters,and a temperature of 87 degree celcius , how many moles of gas does he has? ___mol​


✏️IDEAL GAS EQUATION

[tex]\tt{\huge{\green{Solution:}}}[/tex]

The problem asks to solve for the number of moles of gas. First, we need to identify the quantities to be used. These are

P = pressure

V = volume

n = number of moles

R = universal gas constant = 0.082057 L • atm/K • mol

T = absolute temperature

Second, we need to identify the given values. These are

P = 3.25 atm

V = 29 L

T = 87°C + 273.15 = 360.15 K

Finally, we can now solve for the number of moles of gas. The number of moles of gas is

[tex]n = \dfrac{PV}{\text{RT}}[/tex]

[tex]n = \dfrac{(\text{3.25 atm})(\text{29 L})}{(0.082057 \: \text{L} \: \cdot \: \text{atm/}\text{K} \: \cdot \: \text{mol})(\text{360.15 K})}[/tex]

[tex]\boxed{n = \text{3.19 mol}}[/tex]

Sandra buys 7 lollypops, which cost a total of 7 x 4.00 = 28.00.

She buys twice as many candies as lollypops, which means she buys 2 x 7 = 14 candies.

The cost of one candy is 3.00, so the cost of 14 candies is 14 x 3.00 = 42.00.

Therefore, Sandra spends a total of 28.00 + 42.00 = 70.00 on lollypops and candies at the store.

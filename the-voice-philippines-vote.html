13. . who are qualified to vote in the philippines ?


To become able to vote in the Philippines, you need to become a registered voter. The requirements needed are:

(a) a Filipino Citizen
(b) 18 years old or older
(c) have resided in the Philippines for 1 year or more
(d) have resided in the city or municipality you will register for 6 months or more.

If you meet all this requirements, you can file in your registration at your nearest COMELEC office.

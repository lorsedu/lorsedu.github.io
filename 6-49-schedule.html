17. janice is a bridesmaid for her cousins wedding in New Delhi the wedding is scheduled at 6:00 on the Saturday afternoon and is scheduled to take 6 hours if the time in Manila is 2.5 hours ahead of new delhi and assuming at will take her an extra hour to get ready, will janice be ready for the wedding on time​


To determine whether Janice will be ready for the wedding on time, we need to convert the time of the wedding in New Delhi to the time in Manila.

The wedding in New Delhi is scheduled for 6:00 on Saturday afternoon. Since Manila is 2.5 hours ahead of New Delhi, the wedding in Manila would be at:

6:00 PM New Delhi time + 2.5 hours = 8:30 PM Manila time

So the wedding in Manila is at 8:30 PM on Saturday evening. If the wedding is scheduled to take 6 hours, it would end at 2:30 AM on Sunday morning.

Assuming it will take Janice an extra hour to get ready, she would need to start getting ready at 1:30 AM on Sunday morning to be ready for the wedding at 8:30 PM Manila time on Saturday evening.

Therefore, Janice would not be able to make it to the wedding on time as it would not be possible for her to start getting ready at 1:30 AM on Sunday morning and be ready for the wedding at 8:30 PM Manila time on Saturday evening.

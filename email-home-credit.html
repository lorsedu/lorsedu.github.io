A debit to the income summary ledger account and a credit to the home office account appear in the accounting records of the branch to record the net income of the branch.

If the head office sends money to the branch office, the bookkeeping carried out at the head office is debiting the branch office account, and crediting the cash estimate. The branch office book that receives money from the head office will be recorded on the debit side of the cash account and credit for the head office account. The recording aims to determine the amount of remittances from the head office and vice versa the office must make a record at its branch office, which is remittances, so that it can know the overall amount of money sent to all existing branches.

BRANCH OFFICE ACCOUNTING

Branch Office has the authority to conduct sales transactions. Therefore the Branch Office carries out separate bookkeeping. So both the Head Office and Branch Offices organize separate accounting records. This listing is only useful for internal parties of the Head Office and Branch Offices. For the interests of external parties Head Office prepares a consolidated report namely the financial statements containing the Combined Financial Performance of Head Office and Branch Offices. Unlike the head office investment in the agent's office, which only in the form of initial working capital, investments invested by Head Office to Branch Office covers all initial office needs Branch . Therefore it can be said that the Headquarters acted as Investor (funding party) and Branch Office as Investee (the recipient of funds). Therefore an account is required which is reciprocal (reciprocal) between the Head Office and the Office Branch to accommodate this reciprocal transaction, Office The head office uses the account name of the Branch Office, otherwise the Office Branch using Head Office account. Branch office account is the right of the head office while the head office account is the obligation of the branch office. In making reports reciprocal account consolidation should be eliminated.

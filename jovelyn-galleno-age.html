Answer:

95 200 the invested or the total is 435 200

Step-by-step explanation:

1 year has 4 quarters.

7% of 20000 = 1400 × 4 quarters = 5600

5600 × 17 years = 95 200 earned

20 000 base × 17 years + 95 200 earned = 435 200

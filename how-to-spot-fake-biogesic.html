4. how to spot fake information on social media​


Answer:

how to spot fake information on social media

1. Check the source: Read the source of the information to determine if it is from a credible source. Check to see if the information has been reported by other reliable news outlets.

2. Check the date: Make sure the information is up-to-date and accurate.

3. Check the author: Who wrote the article? Is the author a reputable journalist or someone with a vested interest in the topic?

4. Analyze the language: Be wary of overly sensational or biased language.

5. Check the facts: Check the facts and figures to make sure they are accurate.

6. Check for other sources: Look for other sources to corroborate the information.

7. Exercise caution: Be cautious about sharing information until you can verify its accuracy.

For spotting fake news it is important to consider the source, and to consider clicking away from what we are reading to see what else is on the website to get a sense of who is giving us the information.

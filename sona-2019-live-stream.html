ISANG REPLEKSYON TUNGKOL SA SONA 2019

Noong ika-22 ng Hulyo araw ng Lunes ay ang ika-apat na SONA ni Pangulong Rodrigo Roa Duterte. Dinaluhan ito ng mga mambabatas mula sa mababa at mataas ng kapulungan.

Matapos maipahayag ng pangulo sa kanyang SONA ang ginawa nito sa bayan ay masasabi kung kulang pa. Kulang pa ang kinikilos o isinasagawang pagkilos ng pamahalaan upang masolusyonan ang matagal ng mga problema sa bayan. Ngunit, bagaman ay kulang pa, nararapat pa din naman na pasalamatan ang gobyerno sa mga inisyatibo nito upang matugunan ang mga isyung balakid sa kaunlaran ng bayan.

Sa susunod na SONA ay aking inaasahan na may mga progresibong pagbabago na. Mga pagbabagong kahit kaunti ay nararamdaman ng taumbayan.

A SUMMARY OF THE STATE OF THE NATION ADDRESS (SONA) 20191.) Duterte's Approval RatingsOne of the first things that the President talks about is his approval rating.He states that only 3% of the Filipino people disapprove of him.

2.) War on DrugsThe next topic of the President is in concern to his War on Drugs.He claims that he will end his term with a fight and that the War on Drugs will be relentless.He also notes that the  War on Drugs will not be finished, as long as corruption is prevalent in his country.

3.) Death PenaltyThe President then proceeded to push for the imposition of the Death Penalty on crimes related to drugs and plunder.

4.) CorruptionThe President then proceeds to condemn the Philhealth ghost kidney treatment scandal that occurred in the same year.He commends the National Bureau of Investigations (NBI) for the successful arrest of multiple high-profile criminals. He also reminds the attendees about the time that he arrested more than a hundred officials that were labelled as corrupt by the  Presidential Anti-Corruption Commission (PACC).

5.) Anti-red TapeHe then proceeds to mention the Land Transportation Office (LTO), Social Security System (SSS), Bureau of Internal Revenue (BIR), Land Registration Authority (LRA), and  Pag-IBIG, threatening to kill them if they don't improve the speed in regards to the processing of permits.

6.) Rehabilitation of Tourist AttractionsThe President notes the rehabilitation of Boracay, proclaiming that it was a success, along with the rehabilitation of Manila Bay.He also proceeded to threatened establishments who would violate regulations, saying that he would burn them down.

7.) Telecommunications NetworksThe President then introduces Dito Telecommunity, the third established telecommunications network in the country.He promises congress that no corruption will exist within this new telecommunications network.

8.) Bangsamoro Organic LawThe President then proceeds to address the Bangsamoro government, telling them to speed up their movement so that the quality of life of the Muslim and indigenous people would improve.

9.) Communist ViolenceThe President then proceeds to note of his leadership in the National Task Force to End Local Communist Armed Conflict.He also intends to assign various members of congress to multiple regions in order to aid in his goal to end Communist violence.

10.) EducationHe then proceeds to commend the secretary of the Department of Education, Leonor Briones.He notes the improvements in the National School System, and also acknowledges his support for the National Academy for Sports.

11.) Barangay PollsHe then proceeds to request congress to temporarily suspend the May 2020 barangay and Sangguniang Kabataan elections.

12.) OFWsHe then mentions his signed agreement with Kuwait, in his effort to prevent abuse of OFWs from employers.He also states his plan of establishing a Department of Overseas Filipino Workers.13.) The West Philippine SeaHe then proceeds to claim that he can't assert his territorial rights against China, due to fears of sparking international conflict.He defends the presence of Chinese fishing vessels in the Philippine sea.

14.) PovertyHe states that the poverty rate decreased to 21% in the first half of 2018.

15.) Tax ReformHe then proceeds to address congress in concern to the Trabaho Bill, the second phase of the Comprehensive Tax Program.

16.) Liquor BanHe then proceeds to express his desires to close all drinking establishment by midnight.

17.) Disaster Risk Reduction and ManagementThe President then proceeds to express his wishes for the establishment of the Department of Disaster Resilience, which he hopes will help in the preparation for future calamities.

18.) Fire ProtectionHe then addresses congress in his regards to a law called the Fire Protection Modernization Program.

19.) Land Use PlanningHe then addresses congress in regards to passing the National Land Use Act, which will help in encouraging businesses to develop in places other than the NCR.

20.) TrafficHe addresses the Metropolitan Manila Development Authority and Metro Manila local government officials, telling them to reclaim public roads that are being utilized for private matters. He then orders Secretary Eduardo Año to suspend mayors who do not comply with his wishes.

21.) Rice and Coconut FarmersHe then proceeds to say that he promises to implement the Rice Tarrification Law.He also emphasizes that the coco levy fund should be used with care.He then proceeds to blast the Land Bank of the Philippines for allegedly prioritizing commercial transactions over agricultural activities.

22.) Strengthening National SecurityHe then proceeds to express his support for the passage of the National Defense Act and the Unified Military and Uniformed Personnel Separation, Retirement, and Pension Bill.He also expresses his support for the passage of the Reserves Officers' Training Corps (ROTC) for Senior High School Students.

25. Reaction paper para sa sona ung tagalog po sana​ ung sona ngayong 2019


Last July 22, 2019, Philippine President Rodrigo Roa Duterte delivered his State of the Nation Address (SONA) in front of a thousand lawmakers, guests and diplomats at the Batasang Pambansa, Quezon City.

Same with the previous SONA, the president had a lecture-type speech on delivering his administrations’ plans, policies, programs and initiatives to give way for the discussion of achievements instead of incorporating in the SONA.  

The SONA 2019 started at exactly 5:15 pm and concluded at 6:49 pm, from the originally declared 45-minute speech due to some delays and the expected 'side-remarks' of the president. The president started his speech by extending hand in gratitude to all who kept faith with him in most trying times.

Here are highlights of SONA 2019:  

President Duterte emphasized that illegal drug problem persists and corruption continues but promised to continue fighting until his term ends.  President Duterte wants to reinstate the death penalty for heinous crimes related to drugs and plunder.  The president said that the Bureau of Customs, though corruption-ridden, managed to collect P585 billion in 2018. He also revealed the way some government officials work.He also tasked the agencies with poor services to simplify the process and improve it electronically.He pointed out the rehabilitation made for Boracay Island. He said it was just the beginning and the administration plans to declare the whole of the island a land reform program and give piece by piece to the Aetas.  On the internet-trending Manila Bay rehabilitation, the administration will relocate informal settlers along the waterways and shut down establishments that continue to pollute and poison the waters.  He also brought up the ongoing negotiation of the third telecommunications company.  The president hopes that the Bangsamoro transition will fast-track establishment of regional government for the Filipino Muslim community.  The president praised DepEd Secretary Leonor Briones as more Filipino learners are receiving basic education with over 27 million enrolls from kinder to senior high school. Moreover, he said that the administration plans to create the National Academy for Sports for high school students.On West Philippine Sea dispute, President Duterte said the avoidance of armed conflict and protection of the Philippine territorial waters and natural resources compel the administration to perform a delicate balancing act.  He also made statements on the economic issues of tax reform program, excise tax, the passing Trabaho Bill, and Salary Standardization Law.  President Duterte also urged the Congress to hasten the creation of Department of Water Resources, Department of Disaster Resilience and fire protection program as well as pass the National Defense Act and revive the mandatory ROTC.  

However, key issues such as Charter Change and federalism were not mentioned in SONA 2019, of which according to the president himself is not the proper time to talk about and should be discussed not on the public.

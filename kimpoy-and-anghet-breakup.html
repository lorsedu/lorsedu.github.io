Explanation:

Just change the auxiliary (are, is) into past tense (were, was). Past progressive form can be written in the following manner:

For active voice:

Singular subject/s: was + present participle of main verbPlural subject/s: were + present participle of main verb

For passive voice, add “being” between the auxiliary and the main verb, and change the main verb to past participle. e.g. was being studied

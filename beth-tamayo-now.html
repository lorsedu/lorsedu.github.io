4. beth is 8 year older than her brother. next year she will be twice as old as her brother.how old is beth now?


Answer:

Beth is currently 15 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's call Beth's brother's age "B".

According to the problem, Beth is 8 years older than her brother:

Beth = B + 8

The problem also tells us that next year Beth will be twice as old as her brother:

Beth + 1 = 2(B + 1)

Now we can use the first equation to substitute "Beth" with "B + 8":

B + 8 + 1 = 2(B + 1)

Simplifying the left side:

B + 9 = 2B + 2

Subtracting "B" from both sides:

9 = B + 2

Subtracting 2 from both sides:

B = 7

So Beth's brother is 7 years old.

Using the first equation to find Beth's age:

Beth = B + 8 = 7 + 8 = 15

Therefore, Beth is currently 15 years old.

Beth is 15

Beth's brother is 8

15. Ana is 5 years older than Beth. In 5 years, the product of their ages is 1.5 times the product of their present ages. How old is Beth now?


Let x be Beth's age, since Ana is 5 years older, then
      x+5 would be Ana's age.

In five (5) years, Beth's age will be 5+x and Ana's age will be 5+(x+5). So,
Beth after 5 years = x+5
Ana after 5 years = x+10

The product of their ages 5 years after is 1.5 times the product of their present ages. Thus,
[tex](x+10)(x+5)=1.5x(x+5)[/tex]
[tex] x^{2} +15x+50=1.5 x^{2} +7.5x[/tex]
[tex] x^{2} -1.5 x^{2} +7.5x+50=0[/tex]
([tex]-0.5 x^{2} +7.5x+50=0[/tex] )(-2)        (Multiply both sides by -2)
[tex] x^{2} -15x-100=0[/tex]                    (Factoring)
[tex](x-20)(x+5)=0[/tex]
x=20; x=-5                           (x=-5 is rejected since we have no negative age)

So, the age of Beth now is 20; furthermore, Ana's age (x+5=20+5=25) is 25..

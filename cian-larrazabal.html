Step-by-step explanation:

The formula for the future value is F = P (1+1)ⁿ. Where: [tex]i=\frac{r}{m}[/tex] and n = t·m

The given:

P = P45,000

m = 6 (semi-annually)

i = 0.05÷6 = 0.0083333.. (divide 5 by 100 to get the decimal equivalent of 5%)

t = 3 (years)

n = 18

Substituting the given values, we have: F = 45000 (1+0.0083333)¹⁸

Solve, F = 45,375.

To solve for the interest we use the formula: F-P = I

So, 45375-45000 = 375

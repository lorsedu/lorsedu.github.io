23. andy is seven years older than his wife lori .if andy and loris ages add up to fifty one how old are andy and lori​


Answer:

L = age of Loris

A = L + 7 = age of andy

A + L = 51

so,

(L + 7) + L = 51

2L + 7 = 51

2L = 51 - 7

2L = 44

L = 44/2

L = 22 years old

so,

A = L + 7

A = 22 + 7

A = 29 years old

So Andy is 29 years old and his wife Loris is 22 years old.

Originally, Rizal did not give a title for it and if remained untitled, by virtue of logic and literary tradition, it would be titled by the words coming from the first line of the poem itself. But it was later on entitled "Mi Ultimo Adios" by Mariano Ponce, his friend and fellow reformist.

Mi Ultimo Adios is a poem that tells us how beautiful the Philippines is and how Rizal wants us to learn from our past and to see the truth about how the world view us.

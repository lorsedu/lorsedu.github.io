SHANTAL'S BROTHER IS TRYING TO SOLVE A PROBLEM ABOUT RECTANGLE A RECTANGLE LOT HAS AN AREA OF (x² -8x +7) AND ITS WIDTH IS (x-7). FIND THE LENGTH OF THE RECTANGLE. WHAT IS THE AREA OF A RECTANGLE LOT? WHAT IS THE WIDTH OF THE RECTANGULAR LOT ? HOW DO YOU SOLVE THE PROBLEM? FORM AN EQUATION PERFORM THE INDICATED OPERATION FINAL ANSWER ​


Answer:

Area of Rectangle lot = (x² - 8x + 7)

Width of Rectangular lot = (x - 7)

How do you solve a problem? Form an equation.

l = A/w

Perform the indicated operation

l = A/w

= (x² - 8x + 7) / (x - 7)

= (x - 1)(x - 7) / (x - 7)

l = (x - 1)

The length of the rectangular lot is (x - 1).

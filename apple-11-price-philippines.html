8. The price of apples is 3 for $2.00. How much do you pay for 30 apples? How many apples can you buy for $10?


Answer:

30 apples cost $20.0015 apples cost $10.00

Step-by-step explanation:

The price of apples is 3 for $2.00.

How much do you pay for 30 apples?

[tex]3:2 = 30:x \\ \\ \frac{3}{2} = \frac{30}{x} \\ \\ 3x = 60 \\ \\ \frac{3x}{3} = \frac{60}{3} \\ \\ x = 20[/tex]

30 apples cost $20.00

How many apples can you buy for $10?

[tex]3 :2 = x :10 \\ \\ \frac{3}{2} = \frac{x}{10} \\ \\ 30 = 2x \\ \\ \frac{30}{2} = \frac{2x}{2} \\ \\ 15 = x \\ \\ x = 15[/tex]

15 apples cost $10.00

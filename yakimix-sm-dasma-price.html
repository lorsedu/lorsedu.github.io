12. Michelle went to SM for the 3-day sale. She bought a new pair of shoes and paid only P2,450 discounted for 20% off. What was the original price of the shoes?


First step is to make an equation. We are looking for the original price in this problem, given the discounted price and the discount rate.

Discounted price = Original price x (100% - discount rate)

Discounted price = 2,450
Original price = ? = OP
discount rate = 20% >>> must convert to decimal >>> 0.20

We just need to substitute the values:
Discounted price = Original price x (100% - discount rate)
2,450 =  OP x ( 100% - 20%)
2,450 =  OP x ( 80% ) >>> must convert the percent to decimal
2,450 =  OP x ( 0.80) >>> divide 0.80 both side of the equation
2,450 / 0.80 = OP
OP = Php 3,062.50 is the original price of the shoes.

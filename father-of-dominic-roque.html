2. *Note: Always write dominant alleles over recessive alleles 90 father mother 90 father​


Answer:

Each variation of a gene is called an allele (pronounced 'AL-eel'). These two copies of the gene contained in your chromosomes influence the way your cells work. The two alleles in a gene pair are inherited, one from each parent.

Explanation:

Now we know that an allele is best thought of as one letter, it’s easy to see which is the dominant letter and which is the recessive letter.

Dominant and recessive alleles

The capital B dominates (dominant) the lower case b, so the lower case b retires to a small recess (recessive).

Example: Dominant and recessive alleles can be shown in a punnet square.

NOTE:

In this example the mother has brown eyes but carries one brown eye allele and one blue eye allele. The father also has brown eyes but again carries one brown eye allele and one blue eye allele.

If you have two different alleles, one will be stronger/ more dominant than the other. The stronger allele will overrule the recessive allele. When it comes to eye genotypes, the dominant brown allele will overrule the recessive blue allele:

BB = brown eyes (Both alleles are the same so no overruling here)

Bb = brown eyes (B dominates b to give brown eyes)

bB = brown eyes (B dominates b to give brown eyes)

bb = blue eyes (Both alleles are the same so no overruling here)

Further example

Cystic fibrosis is a common inherited disorder of cell membranes. It is caused by inheriting two recessive alleles (ff). People who have the heterozygous genotype (Ff) are said to be carriers, with no ill effects. Only people who have the homozygous (ff) are affected. The recessive allele must be inherited from both parents.

7. why is san roque national high school named after san roque in antipolo city?​


Answer:

San Roque National High School is likely named after San Roque, who is a Catholic saint widely venerated in the Philippines. San Roque is the patron saint of the sick, the poor, and of those who suffer from infectious diseases. The name San Roque is commonly associated with healing and protection, which could be a reason why the school was named after the saint to symbolize the institution's commitment to providing education and support to its students. As for why it was named specifically after San Roque in Antipolo City, it's possible that the school was established in honor of a local religious tradition or a historical event that is associated with the saint in that area.

Dominant traits are inherited so that explains why it is observable among generations of families. I will give an example based on my observation of my own family

In heredity, there are traits that exist in the genes of living things. These properties are:

Dominant Traits are genes and traits that appear in organisms. The dominant trait will appear, even though there are other traits that are opposite in the individual. In a cross, the dominant trait is symbolized by a capital letter.

Recessive Traits are genes and traits that will only appear in an organism without other opposing traits. On the other hand, if there is another trait that is opposite, the recessive trait will be covered by the dominant trait. The recessive trait is symbolized by a non-capital letter.

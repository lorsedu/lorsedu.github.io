12. Complete the sentence to the future continuous. Debbie Answer (fax) me the form shortly.


The answer is Debbie will be faxing me the form shortly. The future continuous tense form of the verb is formed by using the pattern "will/shall + be + present participle form of the verb". Therefore, using the pattern "will/shall + be + present participle form of the verb", the verb "fax" became will be faxing.

Future Continuous Tense Future continuous tense tells that something will happen in the future, and continue for a length of time. Future continuous tense can only be used using action words that describe activities.

22. what is cost plus pricing?


what is cost plus pricing?

Answer:

Cost plus pricing enable to compute all the amounts plus the percentage to get the final product price or the selling price. It is done by simply adding a markup to the cost of goods and services. To be more specific, in this method, you will add the three types of costs the direct material, direct labor, and the overhead costs for a product. After which, you add the mark up percentage in order to derive the final price or selling price of the product. In addition, Cost plus pricing can also be applicable to a customer contract, wherein the customer refund the seller for all the cost incurred and give money as payment for the agreed profit as additional to the cost incurred during the transaction.

28. 2. In three years, the price of a new model of an Iphone 12 pro phone will be six morethan twice its current price. If the projected price of the new iphone 12 pro is P 62, 990,what algebraic equation to express its current price. ​.


Answer:

6 + 2x = 62990    is the algebraic equation to express its current price

Step-by-step explanation:

Let "x" represent the current price we are finding

the projected price three years from now is 62,990 pesos

let "6 + 2x" represent "six more than twice its current price"

To find x we have this equation: 6 + 2x = 62990

Solve:

[tex]6 + 2x = 62990\\6-6+2x= 62990 - 6\\2x = 62984\\\frac{2x}{2} = \frac{62984}{2} \\x = 31492[/tex]

The current price of Iphone 12 pro phone is 31,492 pesos.

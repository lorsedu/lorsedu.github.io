The Cruz family income is PHP 30,000.

To calculate the amount allotted for food, we need to multiply the income by the percentage allotted for food:

30,000 x 0.25 = 7,500

Therefore, the Cruz family allots PHP 7,500 for food.

B. Brainlying the answer:

To calculate the amount allotted for house rental, we need to multiply the income by the percentage allotted for house rental:

30,000 x 0.20 = 6,000

Therefore, the Cruz family allots PHP 6,000 for house rental.

C. Brainlying the answer:

To calculate the amount allotted for savings, we need to subtract the sum of the percentages allotted for house rental, food, clothing, and other expenses from 100%, then multiply that percentage by the income:

100% - 20% - 25% - 12% - 16% = 27%

30,000 x 0.27 = 8,100

Therefore, the Cruz family allots PHP 8,100 for savings.

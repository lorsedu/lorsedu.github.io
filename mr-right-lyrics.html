19. can you explain this lyrics “in the world full of wrong, you’re the thing that’s right”


Answer:

The lyrics "in the world full of wrong, you're the thing that's right" is a phrase that expresses the idea that the speaker sees the person they are singing about as a positive and good influence in their life. The phrase "world full of wrong" implies that the speaker perceives the world as a place where there is a lot of negativity and wrongdoing, but the person they are singing about is the exception to this - they see them as a positive force that brings light and righteousness in the world. It is a way to express the person is a source of hope and a positive influence in the singer's life, amidst all the negativity that surrounds them.

Girl 1 has 780 friends with which are boys, Girl 2 has 915 friends with which are boys, and girl 3,out of all her friends, are boys. Out of the boyfriends they have, girl 1, girl 2 and girl 3 has 20%, 30% and 5% respectively were not known by them. If the total boyfriends that not known to them is 230, how many friends does girl 3 have? 2 3 5​


800

Step-by-step explanation:

G1=780*1/4*0.2=39

G2=915*2/3*0.3=183

IF THE TOTAL OF BOYFRIENDS THAT NOT KNOW IS 230.

230-39-183=8-THE MISSING THAT NEEDS TO EQUAL OF 230.

USE CROSS-MULTIPLICATION

G3=(8*1/5)5/100

THE 5% CHANGE IN FRACTION TO GET THE ANSWER

G3=(8*1/5)5/100

=40/1*5/100

=4000/5

G3=800

So, girl 3 has 800 friends

lets check:

G3=800*1/5*0.05

G3=8

IF THE TOTAL OF BOYFRIENDS THAT NOT KNOW IS 230.

230=G1+G2+G3

230=39+183+8

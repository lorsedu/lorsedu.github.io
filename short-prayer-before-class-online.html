10. short opening prayer before online class start​


There is a short opening prayer before the online class has started​.

We offer you our study class today. We pray that through your blessing, We will learn how to listen and pay attention to the inputs of our teacher or others. May we appreciate their effort in imparting their knowledge to us. May we participate actively in the class, discussions, and activities to learn more.As we begin this meeting, we come to you today asking for your blessing, wisdom, and support. Let us grow closer as a group and nurture the bonds of community.Give us peace of mind as I prepare for this study time available to us. It gives us insight that we might understand what we are studying and help us remember it when it comes.Head us through this day in all we do, all we say, and all we feel. We will give you our class. We will offer you all the teachers and staff who work in our school. We give you all the children who learn here. We pray that your name is blessed now and forever, in good times and bad, from east to west. Your name is worthy of praise. Your name is mighty.We pray for your wisdom over their lives, work, livelihood, and families. Guide them with your blessing. May they be empowered by your Spirit to see their students' needs and inspire those under their care. We bring our students' parents, guardians, and caregivers before you.

16. short opening prayer before online class


In every event, it must be started by praying. The aim is to ask that the event that we held out run smoothly and be given blessings from God. And one of events or activities started with praying is school. There will be one student or teacher who leads the prayer.

Here is an example of a short opening for prayer.

In the name of God, the most gracious and the most merciful. Praise be to the Lord of the worlds. I seek refuge in You from the knowledge that does not benefit, from a heart that is not humble, from a soul that is not satisfied, and from a prayer that is not heard (not answered).

Thank you for the blessings given today so that we can learn together again. O our Lord, make our knowledge useful knowledge, teach us what is useful for us and add knowledge to us. make my affairs easy for me, and remove the stiffness from my tongue, so that they may understand my words.

May God bless my mother, God bless my father, God bless my sisters, God bless my brothers, God bless my teachers, God bless me and make me to be a good child.

And at the end of the study, we must also pray. This is an example of prayer after studying:

O our God, thank you for what you have given us in today's lesson. Make the knowledge we get today useful. Thank You Lord that you have been with us throughout this lesson And that you are with us right now. Inspire us as we leave this place To love and serve You always.

Indeed, I entrust to You what You have taught me, so return it to me when I need it. Don't make me forget it. O Lord of nature.

17. short tagalog prayer for online class​


Diyos na Makapangyarihan sa lahat, lumalapit po kami sa panalangin sa araw na ito. Nagpapasalamat po kami sa buhay at kalakasan na taglay naming ngayong araw. Humihiling po kami ng kaunawaan para matutunan ang aming leksyon sa online class. Gayundin, patnubayan po ninyo an gaming mga guro para makapagturo po sila ng maayos.  Ang lahat ng ito, pakisuyong tanggapin at dinggin tangi lamang sa pangalan ni Jesus, Amen.

Kahalagahan ng Panalangin

Ang panalangin ay isang regalo ng Diyos para sa mga tao upang atin siyang makausap. Kaya mahalaga na manalangin ang tayo dahil:

Magkakaroon tayo ng mabuting kaugnayan sa Diyos Nagbibigay ng kaginhawan kapag tayo ay may mga suliranin.

Kahulugan ng Amen

Lagi nating sinasabi ang salitang “Amen” sa dulong panalangin. Ito ang kahulugan ng pagsasabi nito:

Pinagtitibay ng salitang “Amen” ang mga sinabi ng nanguna sa panalangin. Ang mga nakinig na nagsabi ng salitang “Amen” ay nagpapakita ng pag-sang ayon sa mga sinabi sa panalangin.

23. short opening prayer for online class


Opening Prayer for Online Class

         Our Lord God in heaven. Thank you for the new day you've bestowed upon us. Until this moment, we still have our borrowed life and strength from you. We worship and praise Your Holy Name. This moment, we will continue to study and acquire new knowledge. May you bless your students with witty brain to think fast and an inquiring mind to be curious on whatever knowledge they will learn today. Most importantly bless them with Your wisdom and a heart that will follow your commandments in order for them to become worthy in their studies. You know that in this recent times, most are suffering from hardships and economic crises. We humbly ask You to bless the parents of your students. May you shower Your abundant blessings and prosperity to each of their homes. So that we can use these not only for our daily lives but especially for the performance of our duties to your Holy name. We hope that you heard our prayer. We ask all of these. In the name of Jesus Christ, our Saviour.

Amen.

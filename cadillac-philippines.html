11. Cadillac Escalade has a mass of 2569.6kg. if it accelerated at 4.65m/s2 what is the net force on the car​


Answer:

Force = 11.95 KN

Explanation:

Step 1: Enumerate the given values.

Given:

m = 2569.6 kg

a = 4.65 m/s²

Step 2: Identify what is being asked.

Required:

Force

Step 3: Solve for Force

Solution:

The formula for force is given by:

F = ma

where: F - force

m - mass

a - acceleration

Substituting the value of m and a in the formula,

F = (2569.6 kg)(4.65 m/s²)

F = 11,948.64 kgm/s² or 11,948.64 N or 11.95 KN

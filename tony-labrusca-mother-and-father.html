30. Tony's mother bought 2 ¾ kllagrams of meat. She will divide it equally to make 4 dishes. How muchmeat will she use for each dish?​


Answer:

the answer is 11/16

Step-by-step explanation:

Dividing two fractions is the same as multiplying the first fraction by the reciprocal (inverse) of the second fraction.

Take the reciprocal of the second fraction by flipping the numerator and denominator and changing the operation to multiplication. Then the equation becomes

11/4 × 1/4=?

For fraction multiplication, multiply the numerators and then multiply the denominators to get 11/16

This fraction cannot be reduced.

Therefore:

11/4 ÷ 4/1= 11/16

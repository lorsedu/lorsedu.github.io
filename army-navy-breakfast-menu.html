1. a language is a dialect with an army and a navy​


Answer:

"A language is a dialect with an army and navy" is an aphorism about the conventional nature of the distinction between a dialect and a language. It points out the influence that social and political conditions can have over a community's perception of the status of a language or dialect. The adage was popularized by sociolinguist and Yiddish scholar Max Weinreich, who heard it from a member of the audience at one of his lectures.

3. what are the differences between army, navy, and air forces of the Philippines?​


The Philippine Army is the main, oldest and largest branch of the Armed Forces of the Philippines, responsible for ground warfare and currently has an estimated strength of over 100,000 soldiers

Their responsibility:
Commands. The Army has 4 support commands, and is responsible for the handling of reserves, creating doctrines and training operations, and overall installation and combat support in the army's operations.


and

The Philippine Navy is the naval warfare service branch of the Armed Forces of the Philippines. It has an estimated strength of 25,000 active service personnel, including the 12,500-strong Philippine Marine Corps. It operates 81 combat ships, 12 auxiliary ships, 25 aircraft and 8 unmanned aerial vehicles

Their Responsibility:
The Philippine Navy (PN) organizes, trains, deploys, and maintains forces for the prompt and sustained naval and maritime operations in support of the United Commands in the accomplishment of the AFP mission.


While

The Philippine Air Force (PAF; Filipino: Hukbong Himpapawid ng Pilipinas) is the aerial warfare service branch of the Armed Forces of the Philippines.

Their Responsibility:
The PAF is responsible for the defense of the Philippine airspace, aerial photography and reconnaissance, close air support to combat forces, development of doctrines and tactics for air operations, participation in national development, and search and rescue operations.

6. 1.Sila ang tinaguriang Sandatahang Lakas ng Bansa.A. LGU, PNP, DEP EdB. Army, Air Force, NavyC.Navy, Army,LGU​


❑Sa talakayan na ito, ating tatalakayin ang Sandatahang Lakas ng isang bansa at ang mga aral na makukuha dito.

Sandatahang Lakas (Army, Air Force, Navy)

Ang Sandatahang Lakas ay ang mga grupo ng tao na nagtatanggol sa kanilang bansa sa mga dayuhang mananakop. Ang grupong ito ang nagproprotekta sa mga mamamayan ng isang bansa. Ito ay itinatag noong Disyembre 21, 1935 sa bisa ng National Defense Act ng Komonwelt ng Pilipinas. Karamihan sa mga myembro ng Sandatahang Lakas ay may mga sandata upang protektahan ang kanilang sarili o ang kanilang pinoprotektahan.

Ang Sandatahang Lakas ay may iba't-ibang pangkat, kabilang na rito ang Army, Air Force, Navy:

Ang Army ay matatagpuan sa kalupaan. Ang Army ang nakikipaglaban at humaharap sa mga terorista o mananakop.

Ang Air Force ay sandatahang lakas sa himpapawid. Sila ang nagmamatyag kung may pangyayating bomba ng mga terorista o mananakop sa kanilang bansa at nagmamatyag sa mga sasakyang panghimpapawid ng mga mananakop.

Ang Navy ay sandatahang lakas sa karagatan. Sila ang nagmamatyag sa mga barko at piratang papalapit sa kanilang teritoryo.

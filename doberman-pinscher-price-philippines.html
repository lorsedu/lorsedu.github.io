Answer:

30km/hr

Step-by-step explanation:

If x is the speed of the dalmation, the speed of the doberman is x + 6.k/h

(x+6) km/hr * y hrs = 15 km

x km/hr * y hrs = 12 km

xy km + 6y km/hr = 15 km

12 km + 6y km/hr = 15 km

6y km/hr = 3 km

y = .5 hours

.5 hr * x km/hr = 12 km so speed of dalmation is 24 km/hr

The speed of the doberman is 6 greater, so its speed is 30 km/hr

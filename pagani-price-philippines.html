1. how did pagani become a highly skilled violinist​


Answer:

After initial study with his father, Paganini studied with a local violinist, G. Servetto, and then with the celebrated Giacomo Costa. He made his first appearance in 1793 and then studied with Alessandro Rolla and Gaspare Ghiretti at Parma. In 1797, accompanied by his father, he toured Lombardy, where with each concert his reputation grew.

Paganini was such a gifted violinist that people think he made a deal with the Devil. This however is not true, except for the gifted part. By the time that he was 13, he underwent tutelage from the famous violinist and teacher, Alessandro Rolla. Rolla easily recognized Paganini's talent, and passed him to his own teacher, Fernando Paer, who later referred him to his teacher, Gasparo Ghiretti.

Paganini was clearly a child prodigy. By the time he was 15, he has embarked on multiple solo tours.

Nagkaroon ng suliraning pangkapaligiran dahil sa kapabayaan at pag abuso ng tao sa kapaligiran.

Sa pagdaan ng oras, pagpapakilala ng modernong teknolohiya ay ginawa itong tunay na madali para sa mga tao upang gamitin ang mga oras sa isang madali at simpleng paraan.

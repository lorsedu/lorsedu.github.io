16. On February 1, 2X16, the balance of the retained earnings account of Blue Power Corporation was P315,000. Revenues for February totaled P61,000, of which P57,500 was collected in cash. Expenses for February totaled P65,000, of which P54,000 was paid in cash. Dividends declared and paid during February were P6,000. Required (1 item x 5 points): Calculate the retained earnings balance at February 28, 2x15.​


Step-by-step explanation:

It seems that there is a typo in the question and the date should be February 28, 2016 (2x16).

To calculate the retained earnings balance at February 28, 2016, we need to consider the changes in the account during the month of February:

Retained earnings balance on February 1, 2016: P315,000

Add: Net income for February (revenues of P61,000 - expenses of P65,000): (P4,000)

Deduct: Dividends declared and paid in February: P6,000

Therefore, the retained earnings balance at February 28, 2016 is:

P315,000 - P4,000 - P6,000 = P305,000

Therefore, the retained earnings balance at February 28, 2016 is P305,000.

4. sara's age is half of her mothers and the total age is 75 years old. how old is sara?


Answer:

Sara is 25 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Mother's age: x

Sara's age: x/2

Total of their ages: 75

Equation:

x + x/2 = 75

Solution:

Find mother's age, x:

2(x + x/2 = 75)(2)    Multiply each term by their LCD 2:

2x + x = 150

3x/3 = 150/3

x = 50  ⇒  Mother's age

Sara's age = (1/2) (50) = 25 years old

Sara is 25 years old.

Check:

Sum of their ages is 75:

Sara's age + Mother's age = 75

25 + 50 = 75

75 = 75  (True)

Answer:

Hey!

Asked: How much will Seth have to pay if she will buy pairs of slippers and 4 crop tops.

Givens: A pair of slippers cost Php150.00 and a crop top costs Php 105.00.

Seth bought 3 pairs of slippers and 4 crop tops

Operation: Multiplication and Addition

Plan/Solution:

Let [tex]x[/tex] be the amount of Seth have to pay.

[tex]150\times3+105\times4=x[/tex]

Apply the PEMDAS rule, (Parenthesis, Exponent, Multiplication, Division, Addition, and Subtraction).

Solve for the product of 150 and 3 first.

[tex]450+105\times4=x[/tex]

Then solve for the product of 105 and 4.

[tex]450+420=x[/tex]

Calculate the sum.

[tex]870=x [/tex]

[tex]\implies[/tex] that Php870.00 will Seth have to pay.

Answer:

Therefore Php870.00 will Seth have to pay if she will buy 3 pairs of slippers and 4 crop tops.

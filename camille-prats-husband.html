17. Emil is thrice as old as Camille in 7 years Camille will be half as old as Emil how old are they?


Answer:

Let's call Emil's current age "E" and Camille's current age "C".

According to the problem, Emil is thrice as old as Camille, so:

E = 3C

In 7 years, Camille will be half as old as Emil, so:

C + 7 = E / 2

Now we have two equations:

E = 3C

C + 7 = E / 2

We can substitute the first equation into the second:

C + 7 = (3C) / 2

Expanding the right side:

C + 7 = 1.5C

Subtracting C from both sides:

7 = 0.5C

Dividing both sides by 0.5:

14 = C

So Camille is 14 years old now. To find Emil's age, we can use the first equation:

E = 3C

E = 3 * 14

E = 42

So Emil is 42 years old now.

25. sino si camille desmoulins


Lucie Simplice Camille Benoît Desmoulins (Pranses: [kamij demul 2 Marso 1760 - 5 Abril 1794), isang mamamahayag at pulitiko na may mahalagang papel sa Rebolusyong Pranses. Siya ay isang kaibigan sa pagkabata ni Maximilien Robespierre at isang malapit na kaibigan at kaalyado sa politika ni Georges Danton, na maimpluwensyang mga numero sa Rebolusyong Pranses. Si Desmoulins ay sinubukan at isinagawa kasama si Danton nang tumugon ang Komite ng Kaligtasan ng Publiko laban sa pagsalungat sa Dantonist.

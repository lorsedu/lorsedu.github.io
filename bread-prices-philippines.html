8. A small bakery sells a variety of different breads and buns. The bread is sold by the loaf, and each loaf is sold for the same price. The buns are sold by the dozen, and each dozen is sold for the same price. On Friday, the bakery collected $360 from selling 45 loaves of bread and 30 dozen buns. On Saturday, the bakery collected $470 from selling 65 loaves of bread and 35 dozen buns. At what price does the bakery sell each loaf of bread?


The bakery sells each loaf of bread for $ 4.00.

Check:

Friday

45 loaves of bread x $ 4.00 = $ 180.00

30 dozen buns x $ 6.00 = $ 180.00

$ 180.00 + $ 180.00 = $ 360.00

Saturday

65 loaves of bread x $ 4.00 = $ 260.00

35 dozen buns x $ 6.00 = $ 210.00

$ 260.00 + $ 210.00 = $ 470.00

10. What final amount would priscilla have if she deposited 80,000.00 for 2 years and 6 months at 9.5% simple interest?


Answer:

99,000

Step-by-step explanation:

F=P+Prt

Where F is the final amount

P is the initial/principal amount

r is the rate(percentage) in decimal

t is the time in years

P=80,000, I=9.5% or 0.095, t=2.5 years

F=80,000+80,000(0.095)(2.5)

F=99,000

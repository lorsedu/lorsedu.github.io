When food is served a la carte in a restaurant, it signifies that it is ordered individually. It is not a course of dishes or part of a larger meal. When you purchase a steak a la carte, you get simply a steak. It could come with garnishes or sauces. Some diners may like to order a side dish with their a la carte steak.

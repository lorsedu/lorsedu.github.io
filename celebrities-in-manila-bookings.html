Answer:

What is Perfect Tense?Perfect Tense is a group of verb tense used to talk about completed actions. The perfect tense consists three category: The present perfect tense, The past perfect tense, and lastly The future perfect tense.

Present Perfect Tense

1. Our guest journalist has written (write) stories since she was in grade school.

2. I have read (read) one of her novels for three days.

3. Mrs. Torres has taught (teach) journalism for several years already.

Past Perfect Tense

1. Father had to go to work by bus because his car had broken (break) down.

2. They drove down the hotel where they had spent (spend) their vacation last year.

3. The parents had left (leave) the room before the meeting finished.

Future Perfect Tense

1. I will have finished (finish) this book by the end of the month.

2. Tita and Tito will have arrived (arrive) from Manila by 10 pm tonight.

3. By October 2020, we will have celebrated (celebrate) the United Nations.

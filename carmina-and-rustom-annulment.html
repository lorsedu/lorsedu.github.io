Question:

Ruben is twice as old as Rustom 9 years ago. Ruben was three times as old as Rustom then, how old are they now​?

Solution with answer:

Let Y = the age of Rustom 9 years ago, so 3Y was the age of Ruben 9 years ago.  Now, Rustom is Y+9 years old, and Ruben is 2*(Y+9) = 2Y+18 years old.  Remember that Ruben was 3Y years old 9 years ago, and now he is also 3Y+9 years old,  So:   3Y+9 = 2Y+18,  if you solve for Y you get, Y=9.  So Ruben is 3*9+9 = 36 years old and Rustom is 18 years old now.#CarryOnLearning

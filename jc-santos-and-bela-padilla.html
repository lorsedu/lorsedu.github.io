16. The ratio of Amy's weight to Bela's weight is 4:3. The ratio of Bela's weight to Chandra's weight is 9:7. What is the ratio of Amy's weight to Bela's weight to Chandra's weight?


The ratio of Amy's weight to Bela's weight is 4:3. The ratio of Bela's weight to Chandra's weight is 9:7. What is the ratio of Amy's weight to Bela's weight to Chandra's weight?

The ratio of Amy's weight to Bela's weight to Chandra's weight is 32 : 27 : 21.

What is asked?

What is the ratio of Amy's weight to Bela's weight to Chandra's weight?

What are the given?

4:3 and 9:7

Solution:

Step 1: Find the 3rd multiple of the ratio of Bela's weight to Chandra's weight.

[tex]9, 18, \boxed{27} \\ 7, 14, \boxed{21}[/tex]

Step 2: The ratio of Bela's weight to Chandra's weight will be...

[tex]27 : 21[/tex]

Step 3: Find the 7th multiple (the 21 to the Chandra's weight because of the Chandra's weight in the ratio of Bela's weight to Chandra's weight) of the ratio of Amy's weight to Chandra's weight.

[tex]4, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, \boxed{32} \\ 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, \boxed{21}[/tex]

Step 4: The ratio of Amy's weight to Chandra's weight will be...

[tex]32 : 21[/tex]

Step 5: It's all equal now so you can join it all.

[tex]32 : 21 : 27 : 21[/tex]

Step 6: Remove the 21 on the left of the ratio because its finding is the ratio of Amy's weight to Bela's weight to Chandra's weight.

[tex]32 : 27 : 21[/tex]

The final answer is...

[tex]\blue{\boxed{32 : 27 : 21}}[/tex]

27. Dr. Lao, a physicist, and Dr. Bela-ong, a biologist, partnered for a research project sponsored by the Department of Science and Technology. Dr. Lao is four years older than Dr. Bela-ong. In eight years, Dr. Lao will be eight times Dr. Bela-ong’s age thirty-years ago. How old is Dr. Bela-ong?


AGE-RELATED PROBLEMS

Problem:

Dr. Lao, a physicist, and Dr. Bela-ong, a biologist, partnered for a research project sponsored by the Department of Science and Technology. Dr. Lao is four years older than Dr. Bela-ong. In eight years, Dr. Lao will be eight times Dr. Bela-ong’s age thirty-years ago. How old is Dr. Bela-ong?

Answer: Dr. Bela-ong is 36 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let us gather the clues that we can use to answer this age related word problem:

Dr. Lao is 4 years older than Dr. Bela-ong. So let us say the the variable x - 4 represents Dr. Bela-ong's age while x represents Dr. Lao's age. These are their present ages.In eight years, Dr. Lao will be eight times Dr. Bela-ong’s age thirty-years ago. This one is quite tricky but we need to analyze this one. After 8 years, Dr. Lao will be 8 times older than Dr. Bela-ong's age 30 years ago so: x  + 8  is Dr. Lao's age after 8 years and (x - 4) - 30 is Dr. Bela-ong's age 30 years ago. In an equation, we can express this as:x + 8 = 8[ x - 4 - 30 ]    => Combine like terms.x + 8 = 8[ x - 34]          => Distribute 8 to all terms inside the brackets.x + 8 = 8x - 272           => Transpose each terms to combine like terms.8x - x = 272 + 8           => Simplify.7x = 280                     => Divide both sides by 7 to isolate x.x = 40

So we already have the value of x which is Dr. Lao's age. By direct substitution, we can obtain Dr. Bela-ong's age:

Dr. Bela-ong's age = x - 4 = 40 - 4 = 36

Therefore, Dr. Bela-ong's age is 36 years old.

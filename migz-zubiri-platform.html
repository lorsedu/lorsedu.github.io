Answer:

Migs is currently 10 + 5 = 15 years old.

Inzo is currently 15 + 10 = 25 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let Migs' age be x. Then, Inzo's age is x + 10 (since Inzo is 10 years older than Migs).

Five years ago, Migs' age was x - 5, and Inzo's age was (x + 10) - 5 = x + 5.

We know that five years ago, Migs was one-third as old as Inzo, so we can write the equation:

x - 5 = (1/3)(x + 5)

Multiplying both sides by 3 to eliminate the fraction, we get:

3(x - 5) = x + 5

Expanding the brackets, we get:

3x - 15 = x + 5

Subtracting x from both sides, we get:

2x - 15 = 5

Adding 15 to both sides, we get:

2x = 20

Dividing both sides by 2, we get:

x = 10

So Migs is currently 10 + 5 = 15 years old.

And Inzo is currently 15 + 10 = 25 years old.

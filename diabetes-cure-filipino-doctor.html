25. 4. According to the International Diabetes Federation, 3,721,900 out of 60,327,000 Filipino adults have diabetes. About how many people have diabetes in a population of 1 million Filipino adults?​


Answer: 62,000 people

Step-by-step explanation: If 3,721,900 out of 60,327,000 Filipino adults have diabetes, this means that about 6.2% of Filipino adults have diabetes. To find out how many people in a population of 1 million have diabetes, you would multiply the total number of people by the percentage that have diabetes. So in a population of 1 million Filipino adults, there would be about 1,000,000 * 6.2% = 62,000 people with diabetes.

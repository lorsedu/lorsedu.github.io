3. 2. Rosanna is twice as old as Jaypee. The sum of their age is 45. How old is Jaypee?​


Answer:

15 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

> Let

Jaypee's age = x

Rosanna's age is twice the age of Jaypee so

Rosanna's age = 2x

> On the other hand, the sum of their age is 45 so

Jaypee's age + Rosanna's age = 45

x + 2x = 45

> add similar terms

x + 2x = 45

3x = 45

> solve for x

3x = 45

divide both sides by 3

x = 15

Since Jaypee's age is x, and x = 15, then Jaypee is 15 years old.

>Checking

x = 15

2x + x = 45

2(15) + 15 = 45

30 + 15 = 45

45 = 45✓

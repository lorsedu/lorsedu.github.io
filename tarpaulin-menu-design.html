10. 2. Mrs. De Vera asked you to order a tarpaulin whose perimeter is 10x2 - 2y .If thewidth of the tarpaulin is -2x2 + 3y. What is the length of tarpaulin?​


Answer:

7x² - 4y

Step-by-step explanation:

P = 2L + 2W

10x²-2y = 2L + 2(-2x² + 3y)

10x²-2y = 2L - 4x² + 6y

2L = 10x² - 2y + 4x² - 6y = 14x²-8y

L = (14x²-8y) / 2 = 7x² - 4y

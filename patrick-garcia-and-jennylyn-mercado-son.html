20. patrick is 28 years older than his oldest son jerome, eight years from now, he will be three times as old as his son jerome. how old are their?


Let the age of Jerome now be x, and the age of Patrick be x + 28

Patrick's age + 8 = 3 · (Jerome's age + 8)
(x + 28) + 8 = 3 · (x + 8)
x + 36 = 3x + 24
12 = 2x
6 = x

Patrick is 34 years old and Jerome is 6.

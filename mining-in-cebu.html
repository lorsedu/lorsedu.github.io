No, I am not in favor. A large-scale mining operation requires thousands of hectares of rainforest and agricultural land to be clear-cut, the use of toxic heavy metals and chemicals, and the extraction of millions of gallons of water, which not only destroys these rights but endangers the health and livelihoods of Filipino citizens, their existence, and the well-being of the environment. This is the issue of open-pit and large-scale mining in general. Mining on a large scale violates environmental and cultural sustainability.

﻿Ang bansang Espanya ay isa sa mga pangunahing bansa na nagpasimula ng kolonyalismo sa buong daigdig. Nasakop nila ang ating bansang Pilipinas nang mahigit tatlong daang taon.

Ang mga unang lugar sa bansa na sumailalim sa kanilang teritoryo at pamumuno ay ang Maynila, Cebu, at Zambuanga. Sa katunayan, ang kauna-unahang manlalakbay na ipinadala ng Espanya—si Ferdinand Magellan—ay dumaong sa pulo ng Cebu.

goldmine [noun] a place where gold is mined. goldmine [noun] a source of wealth or profit.

Does Zambales have active faults? Explain your answer.

No, Active faults within and around the BVAC include the East Zambales and Iba faults; according to the official active faults map of the Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology (PHIVOLCS) there are no other existing active faults in the area.

What is the difference between magnitude and intensity?​

Magnitude is a measure of earthquake size and remains unchanged with distance from the earthquake.

Intensity, however, describes the degree of shaking caused by an earthquake at a given place and decreases with distance from the earthquake epicentre.

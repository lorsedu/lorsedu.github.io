1. A blood type O(ii) mother married a homozygous A(lAlA) father. Determine the blood types of their children.


Explanation:

If the father is heterozygous, there is a 50% probability for a blood type A child and a 50% probability for a blood type O child.

If the father is homozygous, every offspring will be blood type A. The mother of the child is Rh+. She may be homozygous or she may be heterozygous with one Rh+ allele and one Rh- allele.

12. Betty is twice as old as Lala. Three years ago, the product of their ages is four more than twice the age of Lala now, how old is Lala at present? a. 2 b. 3 c. 5 d. 8


Answer:

C. 5

Step-by-step explanation:

Let Lata be x years old.

Age of Betty =2x

Three years ago, their ages were x-3 and 2x-3

(x-3)(2x-3) =2x+4

2x^2 -9x+9 =2x+4

2x^2–11x+5 =0

(2x -1) (x-5) =0

2x=1 or x= 5

So, Lata’s age =5 years

Betty is 10 years old

Answer:

Si Wenceslao Vinzons ay isang bayaning Pilipino na sikat noong panahon ng Hapones. Siya ay isang makabayang aktibista, lider, at opisyal ng Hukbong Sandatahang Pilipino na nagtatag ng mga organisasyon at pakikibaka laban sa mga mananakop ng Hapones. Siya ay nagtatag din ng mga organisasyon tulad ng Hukbalahap at Kalibapi, at nagtatag din ng mga paaralan at komunidad na tumutulong sa mga Pilipino.

10. I AM A SQUARE FIGURED FRAME, MY AREA WAS 121 sq.m. What do you think is the measure of my side? (srry for low rewards, im low on budget :<)


Answer:

The answer is 11m.

Step-by-step explanation:

The formula for getting the area of a square is:

[tex]area = {s}^{2} [/tex]

which s = the side of the square,.

Substitute the area:

[tex]121 = {s}^{2} [/tex]

Now, find the square root of both sides:

[tex] \sqrt{121} = \sqrt{ {s}^{2} } \\ 11 = s[/tex]

Therefore, the value of the missing side of the square is 11 meters .

Answer:

A. 6


Step-by-step explanation:

Let Vic's marbles be x

Joey = 2x

Tito = x+2


2x + x + (x+2) = 26 marbles

4x + 2 = 26

4x = 26 - 2

4x = 24

x = 6

4x + 2 = 26

4x = 26 - 2

4x = 24

4x/4 = 24/4

x = 6

But what is a music video?

A music video is a filmed or video-taped performance of a recorded song. It’s like a very short film that integrates the recorded song with imagery and some imaginings. It is usually produced for promotional, publicity, or artistic purposes.

The modern ones are primarily made to be used as a marketing device to promote sale of the recordings.

These videos were described by various terms such as "illustrated song", "promotional (promo) film", "song video" till 1980’s.

 

Wide range of genre, styles, and contemporary techniques like animation, live action, documentary, and abstract film were being used by music videos’ creators / directors.

 

There are videos that interpret the images and scenes from the lyrics of the recorded song while some creative makers take the thematic approach for the song. Other may not have any complicated concepts so some just add the filmed version of the live performance/s of the song.

29. 2. Vic walked 1/3kilometer to school. After classes, she walk 5/8 kilometer to Manny's house and 34 kilometer to her house. How many kilometers did Vic walk?​


Answer:

To find the total distance that Vic walked, we need to add up the distances of each of the three legs of her journey:

Distance to school = 1/3 km

Distance to Manny's house = 5/8 km

Distance from Manny's house to her own house = 34 km

Total distance = 1/3 + 5/8 + 34

We need to find a common denominator for 3, 8, and 24 (the product of 3 and 8) in order to add these fractions:

1/3 = 8/24

5/8 = 15/24

So now we have:

Total distance = 8/24 + 15/24 + 34

Adding the fractions, we get:

Total distance = 23/24 + 34

Finally, we add the two terms:

Total distance = 57 23/24 km

Therefore, Vic walked a total of 57 23/24 kilometers.

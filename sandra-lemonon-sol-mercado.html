3. sandra is 18 years older than paolo. in 13 years, sandra will be twice as old as paolo will be the. how old is sandra now?


First, we should assign variables to the given facts. Let:

 

Sandra’s Age = X + 18

Paolo’s Age = X

 

In 13 years, they will be:

 

(X + 18) + 13 = 2(x + 13)

 

X + 18 + 13 = 2x + 26

X + 31 = 2x + 26

X – 2x = 26 - 31

-x/-1 = -5/-1

X = 5

 

Therefore, Sandra is now:

 

Sandra’s Age = 5 + 18 = 23

 

Sandra is now 23 year’s old.

 

Checking their age:

 

23 (Sandra) – 5 (Paolo) = 18

29. Jen and Sandra have some beads. If Jen gives Sandra 10 of her beads, they have the same number of beads. If Sandra gives 20 of her beads to Jen, Jen would have thrice as many as Sandra. How many beads did Jen and Sandra have at first?​


Answer:

Jen has 40 beads and Sarah has 20 beads

Step-by-step explanation:

Let J be Jen

Let S be Sandra

Jen gives Sandra 10 of her beads, they have

the same number of beads.

J - 10 = S + 10

J = S + 10 + 10

J = S + 20 ------------- Equation 1

Sandra gives 20 of her beads to Jen, Jen would have thrice as many as Sandra

J + 20 = 3S

Substitute eq 1

S + 20 + 20 = 3S

40 = 3S - S

2S = 40

Divide both sides by two

S = 20

Solving for J

J = S + 20

J = 20 + 20

J = 40

Therefore

Jen has 40 beads and Sarah has 20 beads

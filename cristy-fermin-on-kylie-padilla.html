30. 5. Mayor Cristy Angeles​


MAYOR CRISTY ANGELES

She is a Filipino politician and currently serving as the mayor of Tarlac City in Tarlac Province in Central Luzon Region. She runs under the banner of Nationalist People’s Coalition or the NPC Party last the May 13, 2019 election and she won over former mayor Gelacio Manalang, who ran under Lakas Party, together with Jean Paolo Santos of PDDS-DPP and unbiased candidate Rodrigo Tomaroy.

Her full name is Maria Cristina Cuello Angeles, but preferred to be called as Cristy nd she was born on November 29, 1959 in Tarlac City, Tarlac Province, Philippines. She is married to Victor Angeles together with four childrens.

She studied at University of Santo Tomas or UST with the course of Bachelor of Science in Commerce and have been a Provincial Board Member of the second district last year 2013, but ended at 2016 because she started as being a Mayor in Tarlac City

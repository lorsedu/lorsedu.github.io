19. what is Caged Bird Connection in the Philippine Context


Answer:

"Caged Bird" was published in Maya Angelou's 1983 poetry collection Shaker, Why Don't You Sing? The poem describes the opposing experiences between two birds: one bird is able to live in nature as it pleases, while a different caged bird suffers in captivity. Due to its profound suffering, the caged bird sings, both to cope with its circumstances and to express its own longing for freedom. Using the extended metaphor of the two birds, Angelou paints a critical portrait of oppression in which she illuminates the privilege and entitlement of the un-oppressed, and conveys the simultaneous experience of suffering and emotional resilience. In particular, the poem's extended metaphor can be seen as portraying the experience of being a member of the African American community.

21. A dog will not move more than 10 meters away from its cage. At that time, you aretaking a walk about 9 meters west and 7 meters north of the dog cage. Is there anypossibility of you meeting the dog?​


Answer:

None

Step-by-step explanation:

In this problem, we need to know if you are inside the circle in which the dog may be walking around. If you are outside that circle, then you will not meet the dog.

In all directions, there is a fixed distance of 10 meters that the dog may walk around. If you want to meet the dog, your distance from the dog cage must be less than 10 meters.

To find your distance, we can use the right triangle formula to our advantage to find the answer. Assume that the 9 meters west and 7 meters north are the two short sides of the right triangle, while the asked distance is the hypotenuse.

We have the formula to find the hypotenuse: a^2 + b^2 = c^2, where a and b are the legs, and c is the hypotenuse.

Let's now substitute our values, and start solving for c.

c^2 = (9)^(2) + (7)^(2)

c^2 = 81 + 49

c^2 = 130

c = sqrt(130)

Remember that 10 is sqrt(100). Comparing the two quantities, your distance to the dog cage is greater than the distance where you can meet the dog.

Therefore, there is no possibility that you can meet the dog in your current location.

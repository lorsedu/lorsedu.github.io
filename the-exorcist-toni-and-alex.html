26. a glass of jar has 128 chocolates tony,alex and roy will share the chocolates in the ratio 1:1:2.How many chocolates will Roy get?Maayos na sagot gusto ko​


Answer:

Roy will get 64 chocolates

Step-by-step explanation:

Divide 128 by the sum of the values in the ratio.

1+1+2 = 4

[tex]128 \div 4 = 32[/tex]

Distribute 32 to the values in the ratio

[tex]32 \times 1 = 32 \\ 32 \times 1 = 32 \\ 32 \times 2 = 64[/tex]

So Tony will get 32 chocolates, Alex will also get 32 chocolates and Roy will get 64 chocolates.

Checking:

32 + 32 + 64 = 128

128 = 128

Therefore, Correct.

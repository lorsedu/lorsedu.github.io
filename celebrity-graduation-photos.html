Answer:

Treating each couple as an object, the number of ways to arrange five objects in a line is 5! = 5*4*3*2*1 = 120. Multiply that by 2^5 = 32 because each of the couples can be arranged with person 1 or person 2 on the left. 120 * 32 = 3,840.

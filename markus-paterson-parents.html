4. Markus hiked 6/7 of kilometer.Theo hiked 2/3 kilometer.Who covered a longer distance?How much longer?​

Step-by-step explanation:

Answer:

Markus hiked farther than Theo at 4/21 kilometers.

Step-by-step explanation:

Find their LCD.

LCD = 7x3 = 21.

Change their denominator to 21.

6/7 will be 18/21 and 2/3 will be 14/21.

Markus = 18/21     Theo = 14/21

18/21 - 14/21 = 4/21 (the difference of their distance)

QUESTION

Markus has P100.00 in his wallet, while his older sister

haveP150.00. If they are going to buy MonayBae costs

P10.00 each How many snacks can they buy individually?

GIVEN

• Markus has P100.00

• His older sister have P150.00

• Monay Bae costs P10.00 each

SOLUTION

In order to get the answer, we should divide the amount of their money to the price of Monay Bae, by doing so, we will know how many snacks can they buy Individually.

Markus has P100.00 ÷ P10 = 10 snacks

His older sister have P150.00 ÷ P10 = 15 snacks

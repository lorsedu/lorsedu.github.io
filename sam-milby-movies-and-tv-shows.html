26. Sam bought 24 ballpen then he bought 8 more than five away to his friend which expression shows how many ballpen Sam has now? Math​


Answer:

27 ballpens

Step-by-step explanation:

If Sam bought 8 more than five away to his friend, it means he gave 5 ballpens away to his friend and kept 8 more for himself. Therefore, Sam now has:

24 - 5 + 8 = 27 ballpens

The expression that shows how many ballpens Sam has now is:

24 - 5 + 8 = 27

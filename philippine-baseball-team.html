20. There were 61 athletes at the annual sports fest who played on either the basketball team or the baseball team. If 49 were on the basketball team and 18 were on the baseball team, how many players were on both team?


Answer:

6 players

Step-by-step explanation:

When we add those who played in the basketball team and those in the baseball team, we count those part of both twice.

Basket + Base = (Basket Only + Both) + (Base Only + Both)

Basket + Base = (Basket Only + Both + Base Only) + Both

Basket + Base = All + Both

Substitute the given,

49 + 18 = 61 + Both

67 = 61 + Both

6 = Both

Therefore, 6 players were on both teams.

21. Problem 1. There are 60 students in the Department of Mathematics at the College of Education. One-forth of them are members of the basketball team, and one-fifth are members of the volleyball team. If three students are members of both basketball and volleyball team and the rest of the students belong in the baseball team, how many are members of the baseball team?


Answer:

33

Step-by-step explanation:

Since one-fourth of the 60 students are members of the basketball team since one-fourth of 60 is 15 (multiply 60 and 1/4). On the other hand, 12 students are members of the volleyball team (multiply 60 and 1/5). Then add 12 and 15. We get 27. To know the number of the members of the baseball team, which is the remaining number of students, we just have to subtract 27 from 60 and we get 33.

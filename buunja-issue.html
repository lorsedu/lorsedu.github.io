28. Type of plate bounda-ries transform​


Answer:

3 type of plate bouderies

1.divergent plate boundary

2.convergent plate boundary

3.Transform plate boundaries

Explanation:

Divergent: extensional; the plates move apart. Spreading ridges, basin-range

Convergent: compressional; plates move toward each other. Includes: Subduction zones and mountain building.

Transform: shearing; plates slide past each other. Strike-slip motion.


29. anong ibig sabihin ng Sanhi at buanga.​


Answer:

Ang sanhi ay tumutukoy sa pinagmulan o dahilan ng isang pangyayari. Ito ay nagsasabi ng mga kadahilanan ng mga pangyayari.

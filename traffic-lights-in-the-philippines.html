25. In 1923 Garret Morgan patented a traffic lights with red yellow and green lights that looked very different from today is traffic light​


Answer:

That statement is correct. In 1923, Garrett Morgan, an African-American inventor, patented a traffic signal device that featured automated stop and go signs with warning signals. The device consisted of a T-shaped pole with three hand-cranked semaphore signs, which used red, yellow, and green lights to signal drivers to stop, caution, or proceed with caution. This device was very different from the modern-day traffic light that we see today, which uses round lights with different colors arranged vertically or horizontally. However, Morgan's invention was an important precursor to modern-day traffic signals and paved the way for further developments in traffic control technology.

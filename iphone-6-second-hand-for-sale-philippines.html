12. Ali wish to purchase a second-hand iPhone worth ₱42,990 and the seller requires a 20% down payment. What is the amount of her mortgage loan?pls help​


Ali is interested in buying a second-hand iPhone that costs ₱42,990. The seller has asked for a down payment of 20% of the total cost. To determine the amount of her mortgage loan, we need to subtract the down payment from the total cost of the iPhone.

The down payment would be ₱8,598 (20% of ₱42,990). Therefore, the mortgage loan would be ₱34,392 (₱42,990 - ₱8,598). This is the amount that Ali would need to borrow in order to purchase the iPhone.

20. From the terminal, a bus travels at a rate of 65 kilometers per hour (km/hr). How far will the bus be from the terminal after 3hrs?


21.67 km

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

speed = 65 km/hr

time = 3 hrs

Asked: How far?

Since the asked is how far, then we use the formula distance.

[tex] d = \frac{speed}{time} [/tex]

Substitute the values and we get,

[tex]d = \frac{65 \: km \: per \: hour}{3 \: hours} [/tex]

Cancel the labels and divide the values.

Thus, we get:

21.67 km

29. An e bike experience a net force of 2640 newton what is the acceleration if the mass of e bike is 26 kg​


Answer:

The acceleration is 101.54 [tex]\frac{m}{s^{2} }[/tex]

Solution:Step 1: Analyze the problem.

An E-bike experience a net force of 2640 newton what is the acceleration if the mass of e bike is 26 kg​.

Mass = 26 kg

Force = 2,640 N or 2,640 kg*m/s^2

Acceleration = ?

Step 2: Use the appropriate formula.

The formula of force is [tex]F=ma[/tex] Since Force and mass is given, we will divide both sides by m so that a will remain on the other side of the equation. [tex]\frac{F}{m} =\frac{ma}{m}[/tex] by cancellation, a will remain,  [tex]a=\frac{F}{m}[/tex].

Step 3: Substitute.

[tex]a=\frac{F}{m}[/tex]

[tex]a=\frac{2640kg*m/s^{2} }{26kg}[/tex], cancel out kg since it is both present in the numerator and denominator.

[tex]a=\frac{2640m/s^{2} }{26}[/tex]

[tex]a=101.5384m/s^{2}[/tex]

a = 101.54 [tex]\frac{m}{s^{2} }[/tex]

29. what is a zodiac sign​


Answer:

A zodiac sign is one of the twelve 30° divisions of the ecliptic plane, as used in Western astrology and horoscope-making. The signs are each associated with one of the twelve constellations of the zodiac, which give the signs their names. The zodiac begins with Aries, which is located at the vernal equinox point (the point at which the Sun is located when the Earth's tilt is oriented directly towards the sun, resulting in an equal amount of daylight and darkness) and proceeds counterclockwise (as viewed from Earth's northern hemisphere) through Taurus, Gemini, Cancer, Leo, Virgo, Libra, Scorpio, Sagittarius, Capricorn, Aquarius, and Pisces.

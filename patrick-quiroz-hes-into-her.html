Let n and 3n represent the amount of money in dollars that Patrick spent and Susan received respectively.

Solving the amount of money in dollars that Patrick spent.

[tex]120+3n=2(120)-n\\3n+n=240-120\\4n=120\\n=120/4=30[/tex]

Solving the amount of money in dollars that Susan received.

[tex]3n=3(30)=90[/tex]

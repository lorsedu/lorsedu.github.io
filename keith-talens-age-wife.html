RATIO==============================Question:

The ages of a husband and a wife are in ratio of 14:12, respectively. How old is the husband if the wife is 36 years old?

Answer:

[tex] \large\boxed{ \sf \orange{ 42 \: years \: old}}[/tex]

==============================

Solve:

Let n be the missing age, identify the ratio 'husband to wife' to 14:12. Find the proportion and the age of the husband when the wife is 36. Write it is fractional form.

[tex] \implies \sf\frac{husband}{wife} \\ [/tex]

[tex] \implies \sf \frac{14}{12} = \frac{n}{36} \\ [/tex]

[tex] \: [/tex]

Using Cross Multiplication Method, we can identify the equation easily.

[tex]\implies \sf \frac{ \blue{14}}{ \green{12}} = \frac{ \green{n}}{ \blue{36}} \\ \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \: \boxed{ \sf \blue{504} = \green{12n}}[/tex]

[tex] \: [/tex]

Divide both sides of the equation to 12 to find the value of n.

[tex] \implies \sf \frac{504}{12} = \frac{ \cancel{12}n}{ \cancel{12}} \\ [/tex]

[tex] \implies \large \therefore \boxed{ \sf \orange{n = 42}}[/tex]

21. A man is as old as his wife. Twenty years ago, He was twice as old as his wife. Find their age now.


Answer:

The man is currently 28 years old, while his wife is currently 24 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the age of the wife in the present. The equation should be displayed like this:  

Age of the man in the present = 7/6x  

Age of the man 20 years ago = 2(x-20)  

Solve for the age of the wife:  

7/6(x) - 20 = 2(x-20)  

7/6(x) - 20 = 2x-40  

7/6(x) = 2x - 20  

7x = 6(2x-20)  

7x = 12x - 120  

12x - 7x = 120  

5x = 120  

x = 24  

Solve for the age of the man:  

7/6(x)  

7/6(24) = 28

14. rhea and rhian have combined weight of 125.30 kilograms if rhian is heavier by 7 kilograms how much do each of them weight​


Answer:

Rhea is 59.15 kilograms and Rhian is 66.15 kilograms.

Step-by-step explanation:

125.30 - 7 = 118.30

118.30 ÷ 2 = 59.15

59.15 + 7 = 66.15

66.15 + 59.15 = 125.30

↪️They made a plan to go to the beach but they couldn't do it.

A = L + 7 = age of andy

so,

(L + 7) + L = 51

2L + 7 = 51

2L = 51 - 7

2L = 44

L = 44/2

L = 22 years old

so,

A = L + 7

A = 22 + 7

A = 29 years old

29. Show where a meteoroid meteor and metworsted Are most likely to be found in the diagram below Use the following symbols for each meteor meteoroid andi meteorite


Since I cannot draw it for you, let me just explain it to you.

Meteoroids are space rocks that orbit the spaces between planets. They vary in size from being as small as rocks to as big as 10 meters in diameter. They usally are vaporized when they enter the Earth's atmosphere.

Meteors are the phenomena of flashing light that we see when a space rock or interplanetary debris enters out atmosphere. It is what we commonly refer to as a shooting star or falling star.

Meteorites are what surviving meteroids are called when they are not completely vaporized even after entering our atmosphere.

Age Word Problem

Age word problems are problems dealing with the ages of people currently, in the past or in the future.

Let us analyze the problem.

Given:

Rose's age - x

Cora's age - x + 4 (4 years older than Rose)

sum of Cora's age and Rose's age - 40

Equation:

x + x + 4 = 40

Solution:

x + x + 4 = 40

2x + 4 = 40

2x = 40 - 4

2x = 36

2x/2 = 36/2

x = 18

Rose is 18 years old.

x + 4 = 18 + 4 = 22

Cora is 22 years old.

Checking:

x + x + 4 = 40

18 + 18 + 4 = 40

40 = 40 ✔


22. The sum of cora age and rose age is 40 cora is 4 years older than rose how old is cora now


Answer:

Let's use algebra to solve the problem:

Let x be Rose's age.

Then, Cora's age is x + 4 (since Cora is 4 years older than Rose).

We know that the sum of their ages is 40, so we can set up an equation:

x + (x + 4) = 40

Simplifying the equation:

2x + 4 = 40

Subtracting 4 from both sides:

2x = 36

Dividing both sides by 2:

x = 18

So, Rose is currently 18 years old.

To find Cora's age, we can use the equation we set up earlier:

Cora's age = Rose's age + 4 = 18 + 4 = 22

Therefore, Cora is currently 22 years old.

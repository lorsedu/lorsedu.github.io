#1. 15,21,27,33,...(a15)

Answer:[tex] \textsf{ \pink { \small \: d \: = 6}}[/tex][tex] a_{15} = 99[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]a_{n} = a_{1} + (n - 1) \: d \\ a_{15} = {15} + (15 - 1) \: 6 \\ a_{15} = {15} + (14) \: 6 \\ a_{15} = {15} + 84 \\ a_{15} = 99[/tex]

_______________◇_______________

Given:
a1 = 15
a2 = 21

find d & a15

finding the common difference (d)
d = a2 - a1
d = 21 - 15
d = 6

finding a15
an = a1 + (n-1) d
a15 = 15 + (15 - 1) 6
a15 = 15 + (14) 6
a15 = 15 + 84
a15 = 99

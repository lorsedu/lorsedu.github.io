5. Aida, Lorna and Fe went to the farm to pick Santols. They picked 120 Santols in all. Aida picked twice as many as Lorna did, and Lorna picked 3 times as many as Fe did. How many Santols did each of them pick?​


Answer:

Fe picked 12 Santols, Lorna picked 36 Santols, and Aida picked 72 Santols.

Step-by-step explanation:

To solve, Let x be the number of Santols that Fe picked.

Since Lorna picked 3 times as many as Fe did, she picked 3x Santols.

Since Aida picked twice as many as Lorna did, she picked 2 * 3x = 6x Santols.

Altogether, the three of them picked x + 3x + 6x = 120 Santols.

This means that x + 3x + 6x = 120, or 10x = 120.

Therefore, x = 120 / 10 = 12.

Fe picked 12 Santols.

Lorna picked 3 * 12 = 36 Santols.

Aida picked 2 * 36 = 72 Santols.

Altogether, the three of them picked 12 + 36 + 72 = 120 Santols.

Therefore, Fe picked 12 Santols, Lorna picked 36 Santols, and Aida picked 72 Santols.

Let X be the number of Santols that Fe picked.

Since Lorna picked 3 times as many as Fe did, she picked 3X Santols.

And since Aida picked twice as many as Lorna did, she picked 2 * 3X = 6X Santols.

Altogether, the three of them picked X + 3X + 6X = 10X Santols.

Since they picked a total of 120 Santols, we can set up the equation: 10X = 120

Solving for X, we find that X = 12.

This means that Fe picked 12 Santols, Lorna picked 3 * 12 = 36 Santols, and Aida picked 2 * 36 = 72 Santols.

Therefore, Fe picked 12 Santols, Lorna picked 36 Santols, and Aida picked 72 Santols.

29. Lorna's age is divisible by 8. it also divisible by 12. if Lorna's age is greater than 30 but less than 50, how old is she​


Answer:

Lorna is 48 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

WORD PROBLEM INVOLVING LEAST COMMON MULTIPLE (LCM)

To get Lorna’s age, we will be using the least common multiple.

The smallest number that is the product of two or more numbers is called the least common multiple of that number. For two or more integers, as well as two or more fractions, the least common multiple can be determined. The smallest number that can be divided by both numbers is called the least common multiple of two numbers.

There are several methods to calculate the LCM. Let’s use the listing method:

Multiples of 8: 8, 16, 24, 32, 40, 48, …Multiples of 12: 12, 24, 36, 48, …

The least common multiple (LCM) of 8 and 12 is 24, however, the condition stated that Lorna’s age is greater than 30 but less than 50, and 24 is less than 30. So, we choose the next LCM which is 48.

Therefore, Lorna is 48 years old.

16. Newlywed couple Anna and Kristoff decided to have a semicircular window in their house with a radius of 12 inches. They wanted the window to be laminated with a sunblock coating that costs P50.00 per square inch to apply. What is the total cost of coating the window?


Answer:

Total cost = Php 11309.73

Solution:

In order to figure out the total cost of the coating that will be used, we first need to determine the are of the semicircular windows where the coating will be installed. The area of the semi-circular window is given by  (https://brainly.ph/question/1631280).

A = (π * r²)/2.  

Hence,

The area of the semicircular window can be solved as follows:

A = (π * 12²)/2 ; (r = 12 in, π = 3.1416)

A= 226.195 in²

To know the total cost of coating let us multiply the cost per square inch to the area of the semi-circular window.

Total cost = (Php 50 / in²) x 226.195 in²

Total cost = Php 11309.73

Step-by-step explanation:

Polo = ₱728.30

Sock = ₱150.25

Kristoff's money = ₱1,000

First add up the cost of Kristoff's polo and socks.

• ₱728.30 + ₱150.25 = ₱878.55

Then after that, minus it from Kristoff's money.

• ₱1,000 - ₱878.55 = ₱121.45

Therefore, the shopkeeper returned an amount of ₱121.45 to Kristoff.

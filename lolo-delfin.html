22. Delfin is willing to give a reward to whoever guesses his age this year. His clues state that his age is divisible by 12 and is a multiple of 9, and that he is less than 51 years old. How old is Delfin? ​


Answer:

What is asked?

• The age of Delfin this year.

[tex]─────────────────────────────[/tex]

What are the given facts?

• Age is divisible by 12

• Age is multiple of 9

• Age is less than 51

─────────────────────────────

Plan: What strategy can we use to solve the problem?

Since there are just a few numbers less than 51 which are divisible by 12 and 9, we will use Listing Method and Elimination.

─────────────────────────────

Solve:

Delfin's age is less than 51, so our range is from 1–50. Listing all numbers divisible by 12 within that range, we have 12, 24, 36, and 48.

Another clue is that his age is a multiple of 9. Among the four numbers, we can eliminate 12, 24, and 48 because the only number that is a multiple of 9 is 36.

─────────────────────────────

Therefore, Delfin's age is 36 this year.

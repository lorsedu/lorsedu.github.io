3. Anton Van Leeuwenhoek was the first use the microscope true or false​


Answer:

True

Explanation:

Anton Van Leeuwenhoek is the first man who used the first microscope. He first invented a single-lens microscope that he used to observe microorganisms which he called animalcules, through this observation he provided an accurate description about the bacteria, protozoa, and fungi. Because of his observation with microorganisms and by creating the single-lens microscope, he became known as the "Father of Microbiology".

14. 3. Antonio is 5 years older than Anton, and Norman is four years younger than Anton. If the sum of their ages is 34, how old is Anton?


Answer:

Anton is 11 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's say x = Antonio, y = Norman, z = Anton

We can make an equation:

x + y + z = 34, but we can't find the value of the 3, so we have to make an indirect equation from the given, so we can substitue it to the equation.

x - 5 = z ---> x = z + 5

y + 4 = z ----> y = z - 4

We just have to sub it into the equation.

x + y + z = 34

(z + 5) + (z - 4) + z = 34

z + 5 + z - 4 + z = 34

3z + 1 = 34

3z = 33

z = 11

Anton's age = 11

Antonio's age = 11 + 5 = 16

Norman's age = 11 - 4 = 7

11 + 16 + 7 = 34

34 = 34

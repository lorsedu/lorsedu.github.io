Explanation :

The Senate is a deliberative institution which is usually the upper house of the legislature in countries that adopt a two-chamber system such as the United States, Australia and including the Philippines. Members of the senate are called senators.

The Senate has the task of representing students at the campus level, coordinating the activities of student organizations in the extracurricular field at the campus level, and providing opinions, suggestions and suggestions to campus leaders, especially those related to the functions and achievement of national education goals.

In the Philippine system of government, the senate is made up of twenty-four Senators who are elected generally by eligible electors, as determined by law.

Answer:

The Philippine Development Plan 2017-2022 is the first medium-term plan to be anchored on the 0-10 point Socioeconomic Agenda and is geared towards the Ambisyon Natin 2040 which articulates the Filipino people's collective vision of a MATATAG, MAGINHAWA, AT PANATAG NA BUHAY PARA SA LAHAT.

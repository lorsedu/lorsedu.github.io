5. 2. In three years, the price of a new model of an Iphone 12 pro phone will be six morethan twice its current price. If the projected price of the new iphone 12 pro is P 62, 990,what algebraic equation to express its current price. ​.


Answer:

6 + 2x = 62990    is the algebraic equation to express its current price

Step-by-step explanation:

Let "x" represent the current price we are finding

the projected price three years from now is 62,990 pesos

let "6 + 2x" represent "six more than twice its current price"

To find x we have this equation: 6 + 2x = 62990

Solve:

[tex]6 + 2x = 62990\\6-6+2x= 62990 - 6\\2x = 62984\\\frac{2x}{2} = \frac{62984}{2} \\x = 31492[/tex]

The current price of Iphone 12 pro phone is 31,492 pesos.

16. An iphone 14 in year 2022 was purchased at Php 52,000. If Apple products depreciates it value by 20% per year, determine the ff: a. Resale value in 2025 b. A function that models the value of the Iphone 14 c. Table of values showing the price of the Iphone 14 from 2022 to 2025


Answer:

a. To determine the resale value of the iPhone 14 in 2025, we need to calculate the depreciated value of the phone after three years. The phone depreciates by 20% per year, so after three years, the value of the phone is:

52,000 x 0.8 x 0.8 x 0.8 = Php 26,624

Therefore, the resale value of the iPhone 14 in 2025 is Php 26,624.

b. A function that models the value of the iPhone 14 can be expressed as:

V(t) = 52,000(0.8)^t

where V(t) is the value of the phone at time t (measured in years since 2022).

c. Table of values showing the price of the iPhone 14 from 2022 to 2025:

Year Value

2022:Php 52,000

2023:Php 41,600

2024: Php 33,280

Compute for the MARK UP % (nearest hundredths). MIL%​


Answer:

Markup is the difference between the cost price of a product and its selling price. It is usually expressed in monetary terms. For example, if a product costs $10 to make and is sold for $15, the markup on the product is $5.

Markup percentage, on the other hand, is a measure of the profit made on a product, expressed as a percentage of the cost price. To calculate the markup percentage, divide the markup by the cost price and multiply by 100. In the example above, the markup percentage would be (5 / 10) x 100 = 50%.

It's important to note that markup and markup percentage are not the same thing. Markup is a specific amount of money, while markup percentage is a ratio that shows the profit as a percentage of the cost price. I hope this helps! Let me know if you have any other questions.

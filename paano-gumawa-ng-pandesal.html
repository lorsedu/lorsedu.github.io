17. paraan sa paggawa ng pandesal?


Dissolve yeast and sugar (take 1 tbsp from 1/2 cup sugar) in warm water (110-115°F) .Stir to dissolve and let stand for 10 minutes. Set aside. In a large bowl, combine the bread flour, all purpose flour, remaining sugar & salt. Add the milk, egg, butter, and yeast mixture.  Continue mixing until a dough is formed. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 10 minutes or until dough is smooth & elastic. Transfer the dough to a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth and let it rise in a warm place for at least an hour.After rising, transfer the dough onto a lightly floured surface. Use a dough slicer to divide the dough into 4 equal parts.Roll each part until it forms into a log or cylindrical shape. Cut each log into equal pieces.Coat each sliced dough with bread crumbs and place flat side down in a baking tray with wax paper or into a lightly greased baking tray.Cover the tray with a damp cloth and let it rise for 20 minutes.Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.Bake for 15-20 minutes.Serve hot. Enjoy!

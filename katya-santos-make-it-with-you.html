Biyernes Santo or Good Friday is a solemn day observed by Christians to commemorate the crucifixion of Jesus Christ. One lesson that we can learn from this day is the sacrifice and love of Jesus for humanity. Despite being sinless, Jesus willingly suffered and died for our sins, showing us the extent of his love for us.

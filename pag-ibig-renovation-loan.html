26. Mr. Santos borrowed 500,000 for the renovation of his apartment business. The effective rate of interest is 5%. The loan is to be repaid in full after 36 months. How much is to be paid?


Answer:

He has to pay a total amount of 575,000. The interest is 75,000.

Step-by-step explanation:

Mr. Santos borrowed 500,000 for the renovation of his apartment business. The effective rate of interest is 5%. The loan is to be repaid in full after 36 months. How much is to be paid?

GIVEN:

Principal: 500,000

Interest Rate: 0.05

Time: 3 years

Required:

Interest

Equation:

[tex]I=PRT[/tex]

Solution:

[tex]I=(500,000)(0.05)(3)[/tex]

[tex]I=(500,000)(0.15)[/tex]

[tex]I=75,000[/tex]

Full payment: Interest + Principal amount.

[tex]FP=75,000+500,000[/tex]

[tex]FP=575,000[/tex]

National Service Training Program (NSTP) is a program that aims to increase civic awareness and defense preparedness among young people by developing an ethic of reverence and patriotism while undergoing training in any of the three (3) program components. The Civil Virtue Exercise Service (CWTS) is a program that contributes to the general welfare and the improvement of the lives of members of the community or the improvement of their facilities, especially those that focus on improving health, education, the environment, entrepreneurship, recreation, safety, morals, and others.

The Service of Virtue Civil Training (CWTS) is one of the three components of the State Reverence Training Program, a civic education and defense readiness program for higher and vocational education students in the Philippines. Meanwhile, NSTP aims to increase civic awareness and defense preparedness among young people by developing an ethic of reverence and patriotism during training in any of its three program components, namely the Depositary Employee Training Center (ROTC), The Service of the Virtue Exercise of the Virtue (CWTS), and Literacy.

24. Moon casting a shadow on earth and blocking out view of sun.​


Answer:

The Moon blocks the light of the Sun and a shadow of the Moon is cast on the Earth's surface. This is an eclipse of the Sun, or a solar eclipse. There are three types of a solar eclipse: total, partial, and annular. During a total eclipse, the Moon completely covers our view of the Sun.

26. the school cafeteria offers a pizza lunch choice 3 out of 5 days. what percent of time is a pizza lunch offered?​


Answer:

60%

Step-by-step explanation:

What must be found is the percent or rate of which pizza is offered in lunch in 5 days.

Given:

base: 5 dayspercentage: 3 days

Solution:

[tex]rate = \frac{percentage}{base}\\rate=\frac{3 \: days}{5\: days}\\rate = 0.6\\rate=60 \%[/tex]

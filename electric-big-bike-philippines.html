30. What is the acceleration of a big bike that accelerates from 5 m/s to 15 m/s, north in 3 seconds?​


Question : What is the acceleration of a big bike that accelerates from 5 m/s to 15 m/s, north in 3 seconds?​

GIVEN:

Time(t) = 3 secondsInitial Velocity ([tex]v_{0}[/tex]) = 5m/s Final Velocity ([tex]v_{1}[/tex]) = 15 m/s

FIND: Acceleration (a)

FORMULA: [tex]a=\frac{v_{1} -v_{0} }{t}[/tex]

STEPS:

[tex]a=\frac{v_{1} -v_{0} }{t}[/tex] [tex]a=\frac{15 -5}{3}[/tex] [tex]a=\frac{10}{3}[/tex] [tex]a= 3.33[/tex]

FINAL ANSWER:  3.33 [tex]m/s^{2}[/tex]

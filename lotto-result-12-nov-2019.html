Electrical power and communication services are also predicted to be disrupted on a large scale. Classes are automatically halted at all levels.

PSWS No. 4: The fourth storm warning signal warns that a powerful typhoon with gusts of over 185 kph is possible during the next 12 hours. The storm has the potential to be quite devastating. PSWS No. 5: When a super typhoon is expected to hit a region, this newly enhanced storm warning signal is activated. In at least 12 hours, very strong winds of more than 220 kph are possible. This typhoon is projected to be "very destructive or catastrophic" to the neighborhood, with practically total destruction of structures. Most residential and institutional structures are likely to be severely damaged, with only a few crops and trees remaining. Evacuation to safer shelters should begin as soon as possible because it may already be too late if it hasn't already started. 11. What is the final part of the body of a document?

>> >> b. conclusion

Conclusion is the final part of the body of a document. A conclusion is the last part of something, its end or result. The phrase in conclusion means "finally, to sum up," and is used to introduce some final comments at the end of a speech or piece of writing.

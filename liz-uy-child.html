10. One year ago Liz was three times as old as her brother Jack. In two years she’ll be only twice as old as Jack. How old are Liz and Jack now?


Answer:

Liz is 12 years old while Jack is 6 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

L=liz's age now

J=Jack's age now

L-1 and J-1 are their ages one year ago

L-1=3(J-1)

L-1=3J-3

L=3J-3+1

L=3J-2 (to be used later)

L+2 and J+2 are their ages in two years

L+2=2(J+2)

replace L by 3J-2

3J-2+2=2(J+2)

3J=2J+4

3J-2J=4

J=4 is Jack's age now

L-1=3(J-1)

L-1=3(4-1)

L-1=3*3

L-1=9

L=9+1

L=10 is Liz's age now

Answer:

Jack is 4 years old and Liz is 13 years old


Step-by-step explanation:

Define x :

Let Jack be x years old one year ago

Liz = 3x


Find their age now:

Jack = x + 1

Liz = 3x + 1


Find their age in two years time

Jack = x + 1 + 2 = x + 3

Liz = 3x + 1 + 2 = 3x + 3


Solve x:

Liz will be twice as old as Jack

3x + 3 = 2(x + 3)

3x + 3 = 2x + 6

x = 3


Find their age now:

Jack = x + 1 = 3 + 1 = 4 years old

Liz = 3x + 1 = 3(4) + 1 = 13 years old


Answer: Jack is 4 years old and Liz is 13 years old

Open Your Book by Liz Graf is a story of a girl who wanted to be a teacher. she had a difficult time sending herself to college because none of her relatives was able to finish school. She was still determined to finish school and decided to be a Biology teacher.

Short Analysis

I loved how the fluency of the story works. I was able to visualize the story and the concepts. The flow was neat and the arrangement of ideas was truly presentable. I could visualize what happened to the main character. Liz never gave up on her dreams.

I love the idea that her Biology teacher, Coach Hogan, made a great impact on her career. After graduation, she worked as a substitute teacher for a Biology class at a local school and it shows how determined she is to establish a career in teaching.

When I reached the end of the story, I was not satisfied because it is a little bit hanging. The ending was so good but in a very incomplete way. At the institution where she subbed to be a Biology teacher, the teacher resigned so she applied immediately. Unfortunately, the principal had other plans.

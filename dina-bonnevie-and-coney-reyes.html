4. Dina is twice as old as Dino. The sumbof their ages is 84. How old is Dina?


Answer:

56 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Dina is twice as old as Dino. The sum of their ages is 84. How old is Dina?

Let x = Dino's age

      y = 2x = Dina's age

Required: Dina's age (y)

Solution:

x + y = 84 (equation 1)

substitute y=2x to equation 1

x + 2x = 84

solve for x

3x = 84

divide both sides by 3

x = 28

solve for dina's age

y = 2x = 2(28) = 56 years old

16. Answer the question "what is the difference of windows 10 and the other versions"​


Answer:

What is Windows 10?

Windows 10 is the most recent version of the Microsoft Windows operating system. There have been many different versions of Windows over the years, including Windows 8 (released in 2012), Windows 7 (2009), Windows Vista (2006), and Windows XP (2001). While older versions of Windows mainly ran on desktop and laptop computers, Windows 10 is also designed to run equally well on tablets.


17. Of 50 students, 25 are on the honor roll, 15 are members of the varsity team, and 10 are in the Honor roll and are also members in a varsity team. What is the probability that a randomly selected is on the honor roll or is a member of the varsity team?​


Answer:

P(honor or varsity) = 4/5

Step-by-step explanation:

let A = {Honor Rolls} A= {25}

let B = {Varsity Team} B = {15}

P(E) = P(honor or varsity)

= P(A) + P(B)

= P(25/50) + P(15/50)

= 40/50

= 4/5

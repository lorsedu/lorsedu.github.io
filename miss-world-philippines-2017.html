13. deterning top 3 winners among the 92 candidates in the miss. universes in 2017?


To determine the number of ways in choosing the Top 3 winners where order matter (Miss Universe, First Runner-up, and Second Runner-up).

P (n,r) =   n!/(n-r)!

P (92, 3) = 92! / (92 - 3)!
               = 92! / (89)!
               = 753, 480

There are 753,480 ways to determine the Top 3 winners.

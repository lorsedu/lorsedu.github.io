On sept. 10 mrs. rizalde water meter read 653m. on october 2019 it read 842m.using this data fill up the partial water bill of mrs. rizalde then compute how much he used from september 10 to october 9 2019

To compute for the amount of water used by Mrs. Rizalde from September 10 to October 9, 2019, we need to find the difference between the two readings on September 10 and October 9.

Water usage from September 10 to October 9, 2019:

842m - 653m = 189m

Therefore, Mrs. Rizalde used 189 cubic meters of water from September 10 to October 9, 2019.

To fill up the partial water bill, we need to know the rate of the water company. Let's assume that the rate is P10 per cubic meter.

Partial water bill computation:

189m x P10 = P1890

Therefore, the partial water bill of Mrs. Rizalde is P1890 for the period of September 10 to October 9, 2019, assuming that the rate is P10 per cubic meter.

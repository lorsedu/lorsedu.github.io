3. Meanwhile, this sentence is yet another opinion. "I think" is an opinion marking signals. This is not a declaration but an expression of their thoughts.

4. Lastly, this sentence is an example of an assertion. There is a lack of opinion marking signals here as well. The speaker simply stated it as if they were sure of it.

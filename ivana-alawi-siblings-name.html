11. Doe Let Us Apply! Ivana is driving his sports car at 25 m/s, west when he sees a kid crossing the street ahead. He steps on the brake and comes to a full stop in 5 seconds. What is the acceleration of Ivana's cart Given: Find: Formula: Solution: Answer:​


Given: Ivana's sports car is moving at 25 m/s west and comes to a full stop in 5 seconds.

Find: The acceleration of Ivana's car.

Formula: Acceleration = (final velocity - initial velocity) / time

Solution:

The initial velocity of Ivana's car is 25 m/s west.

The final velocity of Ivana's car is 0 m/s (since the car comes to a full stop).

The time taken for the car to come to a full stop is 5 seconds.

Using the formula for acceleration, we have:

Acceleration = (0 - 25) / 5

Acceleration = -5 m/s^2 (since the car is slowing down, the acceleration is negative)

Answer: The acceleration of Ivana's car is -5 m/s^2.

4. Cheska has 4 purple ballpen, 5 blck ballpen, and 7 blue ballpen in the pocket of his bag what is the probability the cheska will get two purple ballpen with replacing her first pick?


Answer:

The probability of Cheska getting two purple ballpens is 6.25%

Step-by-step explanation:

Total no. of ballpens:

[tex] = 4 + 5 + 7 \\ = 9 + 7 \\ = 16[/tex]

Probability of getting one purple ballpen:

[tex] = \frac{4}{16} \\ = 0.25 [/tex]

Probability of getting TWO purple ballpens:

[tex] = 0.25 \times 0.25 \times 100 \\ = 0.0625 \times 100 \\ = 6.25\%[/tex]

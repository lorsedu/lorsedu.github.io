30. Two people are chosen randomly from 4 married couples. what is the probability that they are husband and wife?


Answer:

There are 4 married couples, which means there are 4 x 2 = 8 total people (4 husbands and 4 wives). We are trying to find the probability that two people chosen randomly are a husband and wife.

There are 8 x 7 = 56 total possible pairs of people that can be chosen when selecting 2 people randomly from the 8 total people.

There are 4 total possible pairs of husband and wife (1 pair per couple).

Therefore, the probability of choosing a husband and wife pair is 4/56 or 1/14.

In other words, there is a 1 in 14 chance that two people chosen randomly from the 4 married couples will be a husband and wife.

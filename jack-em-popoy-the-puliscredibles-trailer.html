28. as you pass a freight truck with a trailer on a highway, you notice that its trailer is bouncing up and down slowly. is it more likely that the trailer is heavily loaded or nearly empty?​


Answer:

If the freight truck with a trailer seems to be bouncing up and down slowly, it is more likely that the trailer is nearly empty rather than heavily loaded because the frequency of oscillation and mass of the system (the trailer) are inversely proportional.

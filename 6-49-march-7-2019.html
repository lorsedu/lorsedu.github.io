3. Question:What is the APPROXIMATE time between November 21, 2019 to March 5, 2019?


Answer:

105 days

Step-by-step explanation:

in one month there is 30 or 31 or 29 days

November: 30 days - 21 days (given)

30 - 21 = 9

December: 31 days

January: 31 days

February: 29 days

March: 5 days (given)

9 + 31 + 31 +29 + 5 = 105 days

3. Devon designed a sign for his company. The sign is in the shape of a triangle with side lengths of 7 feet, 9 feet, and 15 feet. Explain how you know that Devon’s sign is not a right triangle.


Given:
a = 7
b = 9
c = 15

If this is a right triangle c would the hypotenuse
by Pythagorean theorem
where the  hypotenuse or c = [tex] \sqrt{a^2+b^2} [/tex]

[tex]c = \sqrt{7^2+9^2} [/tex]

[tex]c = \sqrt{130} [/tex]

since c was [tex]\sqrt{130} [/tex]and not equal to 15 the triangle is not a right triangle.

28. characteristics of joseph estrada​


The characteristics of Joseph Estrada are ambitious but irresponsible.

Joseph Ejercito Estrada, fondly called Erap, is a popular film actor in the Philippines. He served as the 13th President of the Philippines from June 30, 1998, to January 20, 2001. Estrada gained popularity as a film actor and played lead roles in more than 100 films. In his career, he acted for 33 years. Estrada used his popularity as an actor to gain a political advantage. 

For seventeen years, he presided over San Juan as mayor. Along with serving as Vice President of the Philippines under President Fidel Ramos, he was also a senator for a while. Estrada won the 1998 presidential election by a wide margin over his rivals, and on June 30, 1998, he took the oath of office. He launched "all-out war" on the Moro Islamic Liberation Front in 2000 and took control of further leadership positions and camps.

Estrada served as president for a brief time. In October 2000, a politician's allegations that Estrada had accepted millions of dollars in bribes sparked a corruption controversy implicating him.

27. 2. LJ and Therese help their father fix things in their house. They volunteer to paint their fence- After one hour. LJ has painted 2/4 of the fence While. Therese has painted 3/8 of the face. what part of the whole fence were they to paint an hour?


LJ has painted 2/4, or 1/2, of the fence after one hour.

Therese has painted 3/8, or 3/8 * 2/2, or 3/4, of the fence after one hour.

Together, LJ and Therese have painted a total of 1/2 + 3/4, or 5/4, of the fence after one hour.

This means that they painted 5/4 of the fence in one hour.

Therefore, the whole fence is equal to 1 whole fence, or 1 * 4/4 fences.

So, LJ and Therese painted 5/4 of the whole fence in one hour.

30. rica wraps a rectangular birthday gift for her sister with 5 inches length,3 inches width and 4 inches in height. if she has 100 in2 gift wrapper, will it be enough to wrap the rectangular box?​


Answer:

given

l = 5in

w = 3in

h = 4in

Find:

TSA of a rectangular prism

TSA = LSA + Abases

= [2(l x h) + 2(w x h)] + 2(l x w)

= [2(5in x 4in) + 2(3in x 4in)] + 2(5in x 3in)

= [2(20in^2) + 2(12in^2)] + 2(15in^2)

= (40in^2 + 24in^2) + 30in^2

= 64in^2 + 30in^2

TSA = 94in^2

Rica's gift wrapper has enough capacity to wrap the rectangular birthday gift, since Rica has 100in^2 gift wrapper, and the total surface area of the gift wrapper is 94 in^2

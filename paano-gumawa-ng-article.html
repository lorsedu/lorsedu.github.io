1. paano gumawa ng news article ?


"K I S S " : Keep It Short and Simple
Hindi dapat tataas sa 35 words ang bawat paragraphs. Dapat nauuna ang lead, susunod ang ang optional sentences (na mate-take stand sa iyong sinimulan.)
Ano ang nakalagay sa lead?
-kalimitan...ang dapat nakalagay ay Who, what, so what? also, lead paragraph is limited to one sentence only, hindi na maganda kapag masyadong mahaba.
2nd paragraph- elaborate (when?/where?)
3rd paragraph- direct rotation pertaining to the topic.
Avoid using the words "there" and "this"
Use quote and question leads sparingly.
300-400 words range (buong article)
Each paragraph should contain only one idea (pero huwag paikut-ikutin para lang humaba)
Syempre, malakas ang impact ng title sa readers. Kailangan ang title may dating, yung tipong basahin pa lang nila naeexcite nang ipagpatuloy. (Also, it must have Subject, verb, and object)

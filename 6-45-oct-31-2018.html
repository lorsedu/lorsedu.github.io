27. Gutumin Restaurant started operations on June 1, 2018, with Php 500, 000 from personal savings and Php 200, 000 from a bank loan. During the first year of operations, net income was Php 120, 000. On December 20, 2018, the owner withdrew cash in anticipation of the Christmas day celebration in the amount of Php 10, 000. No additional activities affected the owner's equity in 2018. By December 31, 2018, the restaurant had increased to Php 80, 000. In December 31, 2018 financial statements, how much is the total asset? And how much is the total owner's equity?​


Answer:

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

The total assets of Gutumin Restaurant on December 31, 2018 is Php 580,000. This can be calculated from the initial equity (Php 500,000 + Php 200,000) + net income (Php 120,000) - owner's withdrawal (Php 10,000). The total owner's equity on December 31, 2018 is Php 80,000 which is equal to the amount of increased equity as stated in the question.

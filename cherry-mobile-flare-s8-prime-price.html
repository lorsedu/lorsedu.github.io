8. 8.) 125, 25,5 find the S8​


In mathematics, a geometric progression or known as a geometric sequence is define as a sequence of numbers which has a common ratio, it means each term after the first is a product of the previous term and a constant value (r), r is non-zero number called the common ratio.

Geometric Sequence Formula for finding the nth term

an=a1⋅r^(n−1)

Given

a1 = 125

a2 = 25

Find r

r = a2/a1

r = 25/125

r = 25/125 ÷ 25/25

r = 1/5

Formula for finding the sum of Geometric Sequence

[tex]sn = \frac{a1(1 - {r}^{n)} }{1 - r} [/tex]

where r ≠ 1

r = 1/5

a1 = 125

n = 8

[tex]sn = \frac{125(1 - ({ \frac{1}{5}) }^{8}) }{1 - \frac{1}{5} } [/tex]

[tex]sn = \frac{125(1 - ({ \frac{1}{5}) }^{8} )}{ \frac{4}{5} } [/tex]

[tex]sn = \frac{125(1 - \frac{1}{390625} )}{ \frac{4}{5} } [/tex]

[tex]sn = \frac{124.999}{ \frac{4}{5} } [/tex]

S8 = 156.25

Final answer: 156.25

9. Jana walked across a circular swimming and it is 10ft.across the center.How far will Jana swim around the pool? ____________What is asked? ____________What are the given facts? ____________Solve​


Answer:

The answer is: Jana will swim around the pool for 31.4 feet.

Step-by-step explanation:

How far Jana will swim around the pool is the unknown, so it is what is asked.

The given facts are:

- The swimming pool is circular and has a diameter of 10 feet across the center.

- Jana walked across the pool.

To find out how far Jana will swim around the pool, we need to find the circumference of the circle. The formula for the circumference of a circle is:

C = πd

where C is the circumference, d is the diameter, and π (pi) is a mathematical constant approximately equal to 3.14.

Substituting the given value of the diameter, we get:

C = π x 10

C = 31.4 feet (rounded to one decimal place)

Therefore, Jana will swim around the pool for 31.4 feet.

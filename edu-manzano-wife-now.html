5x = 120

x = 24

7/6(x)

29. A man is as old as his wife. Twenty years ago, He was twice as old as his wife. Find their age now.


Answer:

The man is currently 28 years old, while his wife is currently 24 years old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x be the age of the wife in the present. The equation should be displayed like this:  

Age of the man in the present = 7/6x  

Age of the man 20 years ago = 2(x-20)  

Solve for the age of the wife:  

7/6(x) - 20 = 2(x-20)  

7/6(x) - 20 = 2x-40  

7/6(x) = 2x - 20  

7x = 6(2x-20)  

7x = 12x - 120  

12x - 7x = 120  

5x = 120  

x = 24  

Solve for the age of the man:  

7/6(x)  

7/6(24) = 28

[tex]20 = ( \frac{12 - 7}{6} )x[/tex]

[tex] \frac{120}{5} = 5x [/tex]

[tex] 24 = x [/tex]

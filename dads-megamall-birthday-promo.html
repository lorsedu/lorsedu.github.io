4. 5/8 of the birthday cake was eaten on your birthday, the next day your dad ate 1/2 of what is left. You get to finish the cake, how much is left?


Answer:

3/16

Step-by-step explanation:

1 - 5/8 = 8/8 - 5/8 = 3/8

Note: 8/8 is equal to 1

To get the half of 3/8, divide it by 2 or multiply 3/8 to 1/2

3/8 ÷ 2 = 3/8 × 1/2 = 3/16

7. on rinas 7th birthday her dad gave her 400 pesos if herndad increases her birthday present each year for 700 pesos. how much will she receive at her 18th birthday?


Arithmetic Sequence

[tex]an = a1 + (n - 1)d[/tex]
an = term of the nth term
a1 = first term
n = number of terms
d = difference

SOLVING
a12 = 400+(12-1)700
= 400+(10)700
=400+7 700
=8 100

Therefore; she will receive P8 100.00 for her 18th birthday.

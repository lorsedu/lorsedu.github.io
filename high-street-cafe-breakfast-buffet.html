13. At a breakfast buffet 93 people chose coffe ang 47 people chose juice 25 people chose both coffe and juice if each person chose at least one of these beverages how manay people visited the buffet


Answer:

There are 115 people visited the buffet.

Step-by-step explanation:

Venn Diagram Word Problem

Venn diagram word problems have two or more classifications and a bunch of numbers. The given informations are important to populate the diagram.

Let us analyze the given problem.

Given:

number of people who chose coffee - 93

number of people who chose juice - 47

number of people chose both coffee and juice - 25

There are two classifications in the problem: coffee and juice.

Our venn diagram in this problem has two overlapping circles with the total in each (93, 47). Then, 25 in the overlap as there are people chose both.

(I'll attach the venn diagram.)

Now, we need to subtract 25 from both classifications.

93 - 25 = 68

47 - 25 = 22

Let us add them.

68 + 25 + 22 = 115

There are 115 people visited the buffet.

16. Christopher has breakfast at a cafe and the cost of his meal is $36.00. Because of the service, he wants to leave a 10% tip. What is total bill including tax?


Answer:

Money Problems

Christopher's total bill will be $39.6 since the food cost $36.00 and the tip he had left the cafe is $3.6.

Step-by-step explanation:

To get the total bill, first you need to know how much Christopher had given to the cafe as tip for the service they have rendered. Multiply $36.00 by 10%. Thus, ($36.00)(.10) = $3.6. Now, include this amount with the cost of the food this will give $36.00 + $3.6 = $39.6. Therefore, his total bill will become $39.6.

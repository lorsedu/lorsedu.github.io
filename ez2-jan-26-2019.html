28. solve the exact time of a loan dated December 26, 2019 and its maturity date on March 12, 2021.a. 466 daysb.442 daysc.452 daysd.465 days​


Answer:

442 days (B)

Step-by-step explanation:

> December 26, 2019 to December 31, 2019 = 5 days (excluding the date it started)

> January 1, 2020 to December 31, 2020 = 365 + 1 (leap day) = 366 days

> January 1, 2021 to February 28, 2021 = 31 + 28 = 59 days

> March 1, 2021 to March 12, 2021 = 12 days

Thus, 5 + 366 + 59 + 12 = 442 days or option B.

Answer:

C. Nickelodeon

Explanation:

Nickelodeon, Early motion-picture theater, so named because admission typically cost a nickel. Nickelodeons offered continuous showings of one- and two-reel films, lasting from 15 minutes to one hour and accompanied by a piano. The success of the Pittsburgh nickelodeon established in 1905 by Harry Davis made it the model for their rapid proliferation throughout the U.S.

19. What could have caused the outcry for the cancellation of Nickelodeon's underwater-themed resort project?​.


THE OUTCRY CANCELLATION OF UNDERWATER-THEMED RESORT BY NICKELODEON'S

The resort was supposed to be open in 2020, but its has been canceled because the project would destroy the area’s world-famous marine ecosystem.

Why so? A few years ago, Nickelodeon's wanted to make an underwater-themed resort. But its been cancelled due to the destorying an ecosystem and bring much criticism more than a welcoming one.

Nickelodeon said in January 2017 that they would build an “undersea attraction and resort” on Palawan island that would let fans “interact with the brand and the iconic characters they love”. They said it would open in 2020 and feature restaurants and lounges six metres (20ft) below sea level.

It provoked a backlash from conservation groups, who call Palawan island the Philippines' "last ecological frontier" because of its relatively untouched coastlines and forests. An online petition was held and it has been signed by 260.000 users.

Conservation groups was calling Palawan “the last frontier” because of its pristine coastlines and forests. Which are among the oldest and most diverse in south-east Asia. It was a home to two UNESCO world heritage sites – a subterranean river and the Tubbataha coral reefs.

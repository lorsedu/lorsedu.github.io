22. Jackie bought an iphone for $200. Find the totalcost including a 7% sales tax,​


QUESTION;

Jackie bought an iphone for $200. Find the total

cost including a 7% sales tax.

WHAT IS ASK?

• Find the totalcost including a 7% sales tax.

GIVENS;

• IPhone that costs $200 and 7% sales tax.

SOLUTION;

FIND THE 7% OF 200

7% = 7/100

DIVIDE 7 BY 100

[tex]7÷100 = 0.07[/tex][tex]0.07 × 200= 14[/tex]

ADD 14 TO 200

[tex]200 + 14[/tex]

= 214

FINAL ANSWER;

Hence the total cost of the iPhone and it included sale tax is $ 214

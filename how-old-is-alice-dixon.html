26. John's father is 5 times older than john and john is twice as old as his sister Alice. In two years time, the sum of their ages will be 58. How old is john now?


Answer:

8 years

Step-by-step explanation:

John's father = f

John = j

Alice = a

In the present,

f = 5j

j = 2a

f + j + a

After 2 years

f = f + 2

j = j + 2

a = a + 2

f + j + a = 58

So, if you remove 6 years from 58, we will get the sum of their ages in the present.

In the present,

f + j + a = 58-6 = 52

since f = 5j,

5j + j + a = 6j + a = 52

since j = 2a,

12a + a = 13a = 52

Therefore a = 52/3 = 4 years

6j + a = 52

6j + 4 = 52

6j = 52 - 4 = 48

j = 48/6 = 8 years
